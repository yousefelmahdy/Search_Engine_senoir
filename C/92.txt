      <p>This is a record of material that was recently featured on the <a href="/wiki/Main_Page" title="Main Page">Main Page</a> as part of <a href="/wiki/Template:Did_you_know" title="Template:Did you know">Did you know</a> (DYK). Recently created <a href="/wiki/Special:NewPages" title="Special:NewPages">new articles</a>, greatly expanded former <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Stub" title="Wikipedia:Stub">stub articles</a> and recently promoted <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Good_articles" title="Wikipedia:Good articles">good articles</a> are eligible; you can <b><a href="/wiki/Template_talk:Did_you_know" title="Template talk:Did you know">submit them for consideration</a></b>. </p>
      <p>Archives are generally grouped by month of Main Page appearance. (Currently, DYK hooks are archived according to the date and time that they were taken <i>off</i> the Main Page.) To find which archive contains the fact that appeared on Did you know, go to article's <a href="/wiki/Help:Talk_pages" title="Help:Talk pages">talk page</a> and follow the archive link in the DYK talk page message box.<br><br> </p>
