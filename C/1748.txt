        <h1 class="border-bottom-0">Adding or editing a payment method</h1> 
        <div class="d-none d-lg-block ml-2"> <button class="btn-link Link--secondary js-print tooltipped tooltipped-n" aria-label="Print this article"> <!-- From https://heroicons.dev/ --> 
          <svg fill="none" height="18" width="18" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" stroke-width="2" viewbox="0 0 24 24" stroke="currentColor">

       <div class="lead-mktg">
        <p>You can add a payment method to your account or update your account's existing payment method at any time.</p>

       <div class="article-grid-toc-content"> 
        <h2 id="in-this-article" class="f5 mb-2"><a class="Link--primary" href="#in-this-article">In this article</a></h2> 
        <ul class="list-style-none pl-0 f5 mb-0"> 
         <li class="ml-0  mb-2 lh-condensed"><a href="#updating-your-personal-accounts-payment-method">Updating your personal account's payment method</a></li> 
         <li class="ml-0  mb-2 lh-condensed"><a href="#updating-your-organizations-payment-method">Updating your organization's payment method</a></li>

      <div id="article-contents" class="article-grid-body"> 
       <p>You can pay for GitHub with a credit card or with a PayPal account. When you update your payment method for your account's GitHub subscription, your new payment method is automatically added to your other subscriptions for paid products.</p> 
       <p>We don't provide invoicing or support purchase orders for personal accounts. We email receipts monthly or yearly on your account's billing date. If your company, country, or accountant requires your receipts to provide more detail, you can also <a href="/en/articles/adding-information-to-your-personal-account-s-receipts">add extra information</a> to your receipts.</p> 
       <h3 id="updating-your-personal-accounts-payment-method"><a href="#updating-your-personal-accounts-payment-method">Updating your personal account's payment method</a></h3> 
       <ol> 
        <li>In the upper-right corner of any page, click your profile photo, then click <strong>Settings</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/settings/userbar-account-settings.png" alt="Settings icon in the user bar"></span></li> 
        <li>In your user settings sidebar, click <strong>Billing &amp; plans</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/settings/settings-sidebar-billing-plans.png" alt="Billing &amp; plans settings"></span></li> 
        <li>At the top of the page, under "Payment information", click <strong>Update payment method</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/update_payment_method.png" alt="Update payment method button"></span></li> 
        <li>If your account has an existing credit card that you want to update, click <strong>New Card</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/billing-new-card-button.png" alt="Billing New Card button"></span></li> 
        <li>Under "Payment method", choose to pay with a credit card or PayPal. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/billing_switch_payments.png" alt="Switch Billing payment methods"></span></li> 
        <li>Complete the payment information form, then click <strong>Submit</strong>.</li> 
       </ol> 
       <h3 id="updating-your-organizations-payment-method"><a href="#updating-your-organizations-payment-method">Updating your organization's payment method</a></h3> 
       <p>Only organization members with the <a href="/en/articles/permission-levels-for-an-organization"><em>owner</em></a> or <a href="/en/articles/adding-a-billing-manager-to-your-organization"><em>billing manager</em></a> role can access or change billing settings for your organization.</p> 
       <p>If your organization is outside of the US or if you're using a corporate checking account to pay for GitHub, PayPal could be a helpful method of payment.</p> 
       <ol> 
        <li>In the top right corner of GitHub, click your profile photo, then click <strong>Your organizations</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/profile/your-organizations.png" alt="Your organizations in the profile menu"></span></li> 
        <li>Click <strong>Settings</strong> next to the organization. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/organizations/settings-button.png" alt="The settings button"></span></li> 
        <li>In your organization's Settings sidebar, click <strong>Billing &amp; plans</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/settings_organization_billing_plans_tab.png" alt="Billing settings"></span></li> 
        <li>At the top of the page, under "Payment information", click <strong>Update payment method</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/update_payment_method.png" alt="Update payment method button"></span></li> 
        <li>If your account has an existing credit card that you want to update, click <strong>New Card</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/billing-new-card-button.png" alt="Billing New Card button"></span></li> 
        <li>Under "Payment method", choose to pay with a credit card or PayPal. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/billing_switch_payments.png" alt="Switch Billing payment methods"></span></li> 
        <li>Complete the payment information form, then click <strong>Submit</strong>.</li> 
       </ol>
