                <ol>

                 <li>Tap <b>Account</b>.</li>

                 <li>Verify your identity by tapping <b>Send code</b> to your email address and/or phone number on file. If you do not have an email address or phone number on file, you will be redirected to the <b>Account information</b> page.</li>

                 <li>After verifying your identity, under <b>Download your data</b> and next to <b>Twitter</b>, tap <b>Request data</b>.</li>
                 <li>When your download is ready, we'll send an email to your connected email account or a push notification if you have the app installed. From your settings, you can tap <b>Download archive</b> under the <b>Download your data</b> section.</li>
