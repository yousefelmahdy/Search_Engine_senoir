         <td style="background:#cedff2; text-align:center; border:1px solid #a3b1bf; border-bottom:none; font-size:140%; padding:0.5em"><b>How to request an article</b> </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td style="background:#F5FAFF; border:1px solid #a3b1bf; padding:0.5em 1em"> <p>1. First, check that the article you're looking for doesn't already exist: </p> 
          <ul>
           <li><b><span class="plainlinks"><a class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Special:Search&amp;search=&amp;ns0=1&amp;redirs=1&amp;fulltext=Search">Search Wikipedia</a></span></b> (or use a <a href="/wiki/List_of_search_engines#General" title="List of search engines">search engine</a>) for existing articles. If an article exists, but not at the title you expected, you can <b><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:How_to_make_a_redirect" title="Wikipedia:How to make a redirect">create a redirect</a></b>.</li> 
           <li>Check your <b>spelling</b>.</li> 
           <li>Articles generally use the <b><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Use_common_names" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:Use common names">most common name</a></b> for the subject. This may not be the official name, scientific name, or another name you have in mind.</li>
          </ul> <p>2. Next, be sure the article is <b>suitable for inclusion</b> in Wikipedia. Articles must be about <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:N" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:N">notable</a> topics: those that have received significant coverage in independent, reliable sources. We have a fairly precise definition of what is considered a "<a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Reliable_sources" title="Wikipedia:Reliable sources">reliable source</a>", as well as detailed <b><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:General_notability_guideline" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:General notability guideline">inclusion guidelines</a></b>. </p><p>3. Next, search the existing article requests to make sure your subject is not already listed: </p> 
          <div class="mw-inputbox-centered" style="">
           <form name="searchbox" class="searchbox" action="/wiki/Special:Search" id="ra-searchbox">
            <input class="mw-searchInput searchboxInput mw-ui-input mw-ui-input-inline" name="search" type="text" value="" placeholder="" size="25" dir="ltr">
            <input type="hidden" value="Wikipedia:Requested articles" name="prefix"> 
            <input type="submit" name="fulltext" class="mw-ui-button" value="Search RAs">
            <input type="hidden" value="Search" name="fulltext">
           </form>
          </div> <p>4. Now choose an <b><a href="#toc">appropriate general topic area below</a></b>, choose the best sub-topic that fits your subject, and use that link to go to its page. Add your request there by clicking "edit" at the appropriate heading. Give a brief description, with links if possible, for the proposed topic, to aid others in understanding your request. </p> </td>
        </tr>

         <td style="background:#F5FAFF; border:1px solid #a3b1bf; padding:0.5em 1em"> <p>If you have written an article, you can <b><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Article_wizard" title="Wikipedia:Article wizard">submit it for creation</a></b>. Be aware that there are guidelines about what is an acceptable subject for an article, as mentioned above. </p> </td>

        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td style="background:#f5faff; border:1px solid #a3b1bf; padding:0.5em 1em"> <p>Please help by fulfilling these requests! </p> 
          <ul>
           <li>Help maintain the list by moving misplaced or unsorted requests to the appropriate section.</li> 
           <li>Many requests can be fulfilled by creating a redirect from the requested title.</li> 
           <li>Fulfilled requests should be removed from the list. Remember to <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Categorization" title="Wikipedia:Categorization">categorize</a> newly created articles, and tag them as <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Stubs" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:Stubs">stubs</a> if they are short.</li> 
           <li>More tasks available at <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WPRA" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:WPRA">WikiProject Requested Articles</a>.</li>
          </ul> </td>
