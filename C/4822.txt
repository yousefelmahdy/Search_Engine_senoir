      <h1 class="typography-headline-super column large-10 large-centered small-uncentered small-12">New features available with macOS Big&nbsp;Sur.</h1> 
      <p class="typography-intro-elevated column large-11 large-centered medium-10 small-12">macOS&nbsp;Big&nbsp;Sur brings a refined design, powerful controls, and intuitive customization options to the most advanced desktop operating system in the&nbsp;world.</p>

    <div class="section-content"> 
     <div class="features with-2-columns with-fullwidthrowheader-small"> 
      <div class="features-header-row"> 
       <h2 class="typography-headline-reduced">Key Features and Enhancements</h2> 
      </div> 
      <div class="features-section section-design" id="design" data-analytics-section-engagement="name:design"> 
       <div class="features-row"> 
        <h3 class="features-rowheader typography-features-rowheader">Experience</h3> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Updated menu bar</h4> 
           <p>The menu bar is now taller and more translucent, allowing your desktop picture to extend from edge to edge. Words lighten or darken based on the color of your desktop picture. And pull-down menus are larger, with more space between the lines to make everything easier to read.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Floating Dock</h4> 
           <p>The redesigned Dock is lifted from the bottom of your display and is more translucent, allowing your desktop wallpaper to shine through. And app icons are all-new and even easier to&nbsp;recognize.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">New icons for apps</h4> 
           <p>App icons have been redesigned to feel both familiar and fresh. Icons have a uniform shape but retain the stylistic touches and details that give Mac its&nbsp;character.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Lighter window appearance</h4> 
           <p>Windows have a lighter, more spacious appearance that makes them cleaner and easier to work with. Additional translucency and rounded edges designed to match the radii of Mac create a cohesive look and feel throughout macOS.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Redesigned sheets</h4> 
           <p>Sheets in apps have been completely redesigned, removing borders and bezels to put the focus on your content. They automatically dim the background and scale into the center of the app.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">New and updated sounds</h4> 
           <p>System sounds are all-new and even more pleasing to the ear. The new system alerts were created using snippets of the originals, so they sound&nbsp;familiar.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Full-height sidebars</h4> 
           <p>A new look for app sidebars makes it easier to find what you want and gives you more space to get things done. Easily track your Mail inbox, access Finder folders, and organize Photos, Notes, Stocks, and&nbsp;more.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">New symbols throughout macOS</h4> 
           <p>New symbols in toolbars, sidebars, and controls bring clarity and consistency across apps, helping you immediately recognize where to click. When apps share common tasks — like viewing the inbox in Mail or Calendar — they share the same symbol. There are also localized versions of symbols with numbers, letters, and dates to match your system language.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
       </div> 
      </div> 
      <div class="features-section section-control-center" id="control-center"> 
       <div class="features-row"> 
        <h3 class="features-rowheader typography-features-rowheader">Control Center</h3> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Control Center</h4> 
           <p>Designed just for Mac, the new Control Center consolidates your favorite menu bar items into a single place to give you instant access to the controls you use most. Just click the Control Center icon in the menu bar and adjust Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, AirDrop, and other settings — without opening System&nbsp;Preferences.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Customize Control Center</h4> 
           <p>Add controls for the apps and features you use most, like Accessibility or&nbsp;Battery.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Click for more options</h4> 
           <p>Click to expand the menu. For example, click Display to see options for Dark&nbsp;Mode, Night&nbsp;Shift, True&nbsp;Tone, and&nbsp;AirPlay.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Pin to menu bar</h4> 
           <p>Drag and pin your favorite menu items to the top of your menu bar for easy one-click access.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
       </div> 
      </div> 
      <div class="features-section section-notification-center" id="notification-center"> 
       <div class="features-row"> 
        <h3 class="features-rowheader typography-features-rowheader">Notification Center</h3> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Updated Notification Center</h4> 
           <p>A redesigned Notification&nbsp;Center puts all your notifications and widgets into a single, dedicated column. Notifications are automatically sorted by most recent, and redesigned Today widgets deliver information at a&nbsp;glance.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Interactive notifications</h4> 
           <p>Do more with your notifications on Mac for Apple Podcasts, Mail, Calendar, and more. Simply click and hold the notification to take action or get more information. Reply to an email, listen to the latest podcast, and even expand a Calendar invitation to see where the event falls in your day.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Grouped notifications</h4> 
           <p>Notifications are grouped by thread or app. Expand each group to see earlier notifications. You can also disable grouped notifications if you prefer to see them individually.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Redesigned widgets</h4> 
           <p>Experience brand-new widgets and beautifully redesigned widgets for Calendar, Stocks, Weather, Reminders, Notes, and Podcasts. Widgets also come in different sizes, so you can pick the one that works best for&nbsp;you.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Customize widgets</h4> 
           <p>Easily add new widgets to Notification&nbsp;Center by clicking Edit&nbsp;Widgets. Then customize the size to show the amount of information you want to see and drag it into your widget&nbsp;list.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Discover more third-party widgets</h4> 
           <p>Discover new third-party widgets for Notification&nbsp;Center on the App&nbsp;Store.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
       </div>

      <div class="features-section section-safari" id="safari" data-analytics-section-engagement="name:safari"> 
       <div class="features-row"> 
        <h3 class="features-rowheader typography-features-rowheader">Safari</h3> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Customizable start page</h4> 
           <p>Customize the new start page to make it your own. You can set a background image and select new sections to display on the start page — including Favorites, Siri Suggestions, Reading List, iCloud Tabs, and even a Privacy Report. You can also rearrange the sections using the customization&nbsp;controls.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Improved performance</h4> 
           <p>Safari was already the fastest desktop browser — and now it’s even faster. Safari loads frequently visited sites 50 percent faster on average than&nbsp;Chrome.<sup class="footnote footnote-number"><a href="#footnote-1" aria-label="Footnote 1">1</a></sup></p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Improved power efficiency</h4> 
           <p>Safari is optimized specifically for Mac, so it’s more efficient than other browsers on macOS. Stream video for up to one and a half hours longer and browse the web for up to one hour longer on a Mac notebook compared with Chrome or Firefox.<sup class="footnote footnote-number"><a href="#footnote-2" aria-label="Footnote 2">2</a></sup></p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Favicons in tabs</h4> 
           <p>Default tab favicons help you easily identify open tabs at a glance.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">See more tabs at once</h4> 
           <p>The new tab bar design displays more tabs onscreen at once so you can quickly move between&nbsp;them.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Website previews</h4> 
           <p>When you just want a peek at what’s on a tab, hold the pointer over it to get a quick preview of the page.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> <span class="feature-violator violator-frameless violator-frameless-reduced">Beta</span> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Translation</h4> 
           <p>Translate entire web pages in Safari.<sup class="footnote footnote-number"><a href="#footnote-3" aria-label="Footnote 3">3</a></sup> When you encounter a compatible page, just click the translation icon in the address field to translate into English, Spanish, Chinese, French, German, Russian, or Brazilian&nbsp;Portuguese.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Safari extensions on the App&nbsp;Store</h4> 
           <p>An all-new extensions category on the App&nbsp;Store showcases Safari extensions with editorial spotlights and top charts, making it easier to discover great extensions from developers. All extensions are reviewed, signed, and hosted by Apple for your&nbsp;security.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">WebExtensions API support</h4> 
           <p>New support for the WebExtensions&nbsp;API and migration tools allows developers to bring Chrome extensions to Safari —&nbsp;letting you personalize your browsing experience with new Safari extensions from your favorite&nbsp;developers.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Web Speech API</h4> 
           <p>New support for the Web&nbsp;Speech&nbsp;API allows web developers to incorporate speech recognition into their web pages, enabling real-time captioning of calls and video, dictating email, and navigating websites with your voice.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Grant extensions website access</h4> 
           <p>Keep the websites you visit and tabs you use even more private. Safari asks you which websites a Safari extension can work with — and you can grant access for one day or all the time.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Privacy Report</h4> 
           <p>Safari uses Intelligent Tracking Prevention to identify trackers and help prevent them from profiling or following you around the web. A new Privacy Report shows how Safari protects your browsing across every website you visit. Go to the Privacy Report from your Safari menu and you’ll get an in-depth overview of every cross-site tracker prevented over the last 30&nbsp;days.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Privacy Report for each website</h4> 
           <p>See the privacy practices for every website you visit. Just click the Privacy Report button in your Safari toolbar for a summary of all cross-site trackers prevented by Intelligent Tracking&nbsp;Prevention.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Start page Privacy Report</h4> 
           <p>Add a Privacy Report to your start page — so every time you open a new window or tab, you can see how Safari protects your&nbsp;privacy.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Import passwords and settings from&nbsp;Chrome</h4> 
           <p>Easily import history, bookmarks, and saved passwords from Chrome into&nbsp;Safari.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="features-column"> 
         <div class="copy-wrapper"> 
          <div class="typography-body"> 
           <h4 class="feature-headline typography-feature-headline">Password monitoring</h4> 
           <p>Safari securely monitors your passwords, automatically keeping an eye out for any saved passwords that may have been involved in a data breach. If a breach is discovered, Safari helps you upgrade existing passwords and even generates secure passwords automatically. Safari keeps your data private and doesn’t reveal your password information to anyone — even Apple.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
       </div>
