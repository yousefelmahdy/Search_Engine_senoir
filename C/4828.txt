      <h2 class="typography-headline-reduced">Upgrading is free and easy</h2> 
      <h4>Upgrading from macOS&nbsp;Catalina 10.15 or Mojave 10.14?</h4> 
      <p>Go to Software Update in System&nbsp;Preferences to find macOS&nbsp;Big&nbsp;Sur. Click Upgrade Now and follow the onscreen instructions.</p> 
      <h4>Upgrading from an older version of macOS?</h4> 
      <p>If you’re running any release from macOS 10.13 to 10.9, you can upgrade to macOS&nbsp;Big&nbsp;Sur from the App&nbsp;Store. If you’re running Mountain Lion 10.8, you will need to upgrade to El&nbsp;Capitan&nbsp;10.11&nbsp;first.</p> 
      <p>If you don’t have broadband access, you can upgrade your Mac at any Apple&nbsp;Store.</p> 
      <p><a class="more" href="https://support.apple.com/macos" data-analytics-title="learn more about how to upgrade to macos big sur" data-analytics-exit-link>Learn more about how&nbsp;to&nbsp;upgrade</a></p>

      <h3 class="typography-label">Feature Requirements</h3> 
     </div> 
     <div class="column large-4 large-push-1 medium-7 medium-push-2 small-12 small-push-0"> 
      <h4 class="small-margin">Siri</h4> 
      <p>Requires a broadband internet connection and microphone (built-in or external). </p> 
      <h4 class="small-margin">Hey Siri</h4> 
      <p>Supported by the following Mac&nbsp;models: </p>
      <ul class="stack"> 
       <li>MacBook&nbsp;Pro&nbsp;(2018 or later)</li> 
       <li>MacBook&nbsp;Air&nbsp;(2018 or later)</li> 
       <li>iMac&nbsp;Pro (2017 or later)</li> 
      </ul> 
      <p></p> 
      <h4>Dictation, Voice Control, and Voice Memos</h4> 
      <p>Requires a microphone (built-in or external).</p> 
      <h4>Spotlight Suggestions</h4> 
      <p>Requires a broadband internet connection.</p> 
      <h4>Gestures</h4> 
      <p>Requires a Multi-Touch trackpad, Force Touch trackpad, Magic&nbsp;Trackpad, or Magic&nbsp;Mouse.</p> 
      <p>Force Touch gestures require a <span class="nowrap">Force Touch trackpad.</span></p> 
      <p>VoiceOver gestures require a Multi-Touch trackpad, Force Touch trackpad, or Magic&nbsp;Trackpad.</p> 
      <h4>Photo Booth</h4> 
      <p>Requires a FaceTime or iSight camera (built-in or external) or USB video class (UVC) camera.</p> 
      <h4>FaceTime</h4> 
      <p>Audio calls require a microphone (built-in or external) and broadband internet&nbsp;connection.</p> 
      <p>Video calls require a built-in FaceTime&nbsp;camera, an iSight&nbsp;camera (built-in or external), or a USB video class (UVC)&nbsp;camera; and broadband internet&nbsp;connection.</p> 
      <h4 class="small-margin">Apple TV</h4> 
      <p>High dynamic range (HDR) video playback is supported by the following Mac models: </p>
      <ul class="stack"> 
       <li>MacBook&nbsp;Pro&nbsp;(2018 or later)</li> 
       <li>iMac&nbsp;Pro (2017 or later)</li> 
       <li>Mac&nbsp;Pro (2019) with Pro Display XDR</li> 
      </ul> 
      <p></p> 
      <p>Dolby Atmos soundtrack playback is supported by the following Mac models: </p>
      <ul class="stack"> 
       <li>MacBook&nbsp;Air&nbsp;(2018 or later)</li> 
       <li>MacBook&nbsp;Pro&nbsp;(2018 or later)</li> 
      </ul> 
      <p></p> 
      <h4 class="small-margin">Sidecar</h4> 
      <p>Supported by the following Mac models: </p>
      <ul class="stack"> 
       <li>MacBook (2016 or later)</li> 
       <li>MacBook Air (2018 or later)</li> 
       <li>MacBook&nbsp;Pro&nbsp;(2016 or later)</li> 
       <li>Mac mini (2018 or later)</li> 
       <li>iMac (late 2015 or later)</li> 
       <li>iMac&nbsp;Pro (2017 or later)</li> 
       <li>Mac&nbsp;Pro (2019)</li> 
      </ul> 
      <p></p> 
      <p>Supported by all iPad models with Apple&nbsp;Pencil support: </p>
      <ul class="stack"> 
       <li>12.9-inch iPad Pro</li> 
       <li>11-inch iPad Pro</li> 
       <li>10.5-inch iPad Pro</li> 
       <li>9.7-inch iPad Pro</li> 
       <li>iPad (6th generation or later)</li> 
       <li>iPad mini (5th generation)</li> 
       <li>iPad Air (3rd and 4th generation)</li> 
      </ul> 
      <p></p> 
      <h4>Continuity&nbsp;Camera</h4> 
      <p>Requires an iPhone or iPad that supports iOS 12 or later.</p> 
      <h4>Continuity Sketch and Continuity&nbsp;Markup</h4> 
      <p>Requires an iPhone with iOS 13 or&nbsp;later or an iPad with iPadOS 13 or&nbsp;later.</p> 
      <h4>Handoff</h4> 
      <p>Requires an iPhone or iPad with a Lightning connector or with USB-C and iOS&nbsp;8 or later.</p> 
      <h4>Instant Hotspot</h4> 
      <p>Requires an iPhone or iPad with cellular connectivity, a Lightning connector or USB-C, and iOS&nbsp;8.1 or later. Requires Personal Hotspot service through your&nbsp;carrier.</p> 
     </div> 
     <div class="column large-4 large-push-2 medium-7 medium-push-5 small-12 small-push-0"> 
      <h4 class="feature-top-margin">Universal Clipboard</h4> 
      <p>Requires an iPhone or iPad with a Lightning connector or with USB-C and iOS&nbsp;10 or&nbsp;later.</p> 
      <h4>Auto Unlock</h4> 
      <p>Requires an Apple&nbsp;Watch with watchOS&nbsp;3 or later or an iPhone&nbsp;5 or&nbsp;later.</p> 
      <h4>Approve with Apple Watch</h4> 
      <p>Requires an Apple&nbsp;Watch with watchOS&nbsp;6 or later or an iPhone&nbsp;6s or later with iOS&nbsp;13 or&nbsp;later.</p> 
      <h4>Apple&nbsp;Pay on the Web</h4> 
      <p>Requires a MacBook Pro or MacBook Air with Touch&nbsp;ID, an iPhone 6 or later with iOS&nbsp;10 or later, or an Apple Watch with watchOS&nbsp;3 or&nbsp;later.</p> 
      <h4>Phone Calling</h4> 
      <p>Requires an iPhone with iOS&nbsp;8 or later and an activated carrier plan.</p> 
      <h4>SMS</h4> 
      <p>Requires an iPhone with iOS&nbsp;8.1 or later and an activated carrier plan.</p> 
      <h4>Home</h4> 
      <p>Requires an iPhone with iOS&nbsp;12 or later and a configured Home&nbsp;app.</p> 
      <h4 class="small-margin extra">AirDrop</h4> 
      <p>AirDrop to iOS and iPadOS devices requires an iPhone or iPad with a Lightning connector or with USB-C and iOS&nbsp;7 or later.</p> 
      <h4>AirPlay</h4> 
      <p>AirPlay Mirroring requires an Apple&nbsp;TV (2nd generation or later).</p> 
      <p>AirPlay for web video requires an Apple&nbsp;TV (2nd generation or later).</p> 
      <p>Peer-to-peer AirPlay requires a Mac (2012 or later) and an Apple&nbsp;TV (3rd generation rev A, model A1469 or later) with Apple&nbsp;TV software 7.0 or later.</p> 
      <h4>Time Machine</h4> 
      <p>Requires an external storage device (sold&nbsp;separately).</p> 
      <p class="cta"><a href="https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT202784" class="more" aria-label="Learn more about Time Machine" data-analytics-title="learn more about time machine" data-analytics-exit-link>Learn&nbsp;more</a></p> 
      <h4>Maps electric vehicle routing</h4> 
      <p>Requires an iPhone with iOS&nbsp;14 and a compatible electric vehicle.</p> 
      <h4>Maps license plate restrictions</h4> 
      <p>Requires an iPhone running iOS 14 or an iPad running iPadOS 14.</p> 
      <h4>Boot Camp</h4> 
      <p>Allows Boot Camp installations of Windows&nbsp;10 on supported Mac&nbsp;models.</p> 
      <p class="cta"><a href="https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT204990" class="more" aria-label="Learn more about Boot Camp" data-analytics-title="learn more about boot camp" data-analytics-exit-link>Learn&nbsp;more</a></p> 
      <h4>Exchange Support</h4> 
      <p>Requires Microsoft Office 365, Exchange 2016, Exchange 2013, or Exchange Server 2010. Installing the latest Service Packs is recommended.</p> 
      <h4>Windows Migration</h4> 
      <p>Supports OS X 10.7 or later and Windows&nbsp;7 or later.</p> 
      <h4>App&nbsp;Store</h4> 
      <p>Available only to persons age 13 or older in the U.S. and many other countries and&nbsp;regions.</p> 
      <h4>Photos</h4> 
      <p>The improved Retouch tool is supported on the following Mac models:</p> 
      <ul class="stack"> 
       <li>MacBook Pro (15-inch and 16-inch models) introduced in 2016 or later</li> 
       <li>iMac (Retina 5K models) introduced in 2014 or later</li> 
       <li>iMac (Retina 4K models) introduced in 2017 or later</li> 
       <li>iMac Pro (2017 or later)</li> 
       <li>Mac Pro introduced in 2013 or later</li> 
      </ul>
