        <p>Welcome to <b>WikiProject Cats</b>, a <a href="/wiki/Wikiproject" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikiproject">wikiproject</a> dedicated to improving to Wikipedia's articles relating to the family of mammals known as the <a href="/wiki/Felidae" title="Felidae">Felidae</a> and commonly referred to as "cats". Any editor can help contribute to this project by looking at the tasks listed here to find something in their interest or inquire on the <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia_talk:WikiProject_Cats" title="Wikipedia talk:WikiProject Cats">talk page</a>. Anyone seeking to join should add their name to the <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Cats/Participants" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Cats/Participants">list of active participants</a>. </p>

        <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Purpose">Purpose</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wikipedia:WikiProject_Cats&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Purpose">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
        <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Goals">Goals</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wikipedia:WikiProject_Cats&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Goals">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3> 
        <ul>
         <li>To improve Wikipedia's coverage of cats by creating, expanding, and maintaining articles.</li> 
         <li>To serve as the central point of cat-related article discussion.</li> 
         <li>To provide guidelines and recommendations for articles about cats.</li>
        </ul> 
        <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Scope">Scope</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wikipedia:WikiProject_Cats&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Scope">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3> 
        <p>This project deals with the creation and editing of articles related to cats, both wild species and domestic breeds. We shall also include within the scope of this project those articles which have a direct and significant impact upon all members of the family <a href="/wiki/Felidae" title="Felidae">Felidae</a>, including such things as feline diseases and welfare. </p> 
        <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Parent_project">Parent project</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wikipedia:WikiProject_Cats&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Parent project">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3> 
        <ul>
         <li><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Mammals" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Mammals">Wikipedia:WikiProject Mammals</a></li> 
         <li>See also: <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Veterinary_medicine" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Veterinary medicine">WikiProject Veterinary medicine</a></li>
        </ul> 
        <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Departments">Departments</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wikipedia:WikiProject_Cats&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Departments">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
        <ul>
         <li>The <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Cats/Assessment" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Cats/Assessment">Assessment</a> department monitors the quality of articles and indicates the relative importance of articles to this project. It is very useful in determining which articles are most in need of further attention and which are not.</li> 
         <li>The <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Cats/Peer_review" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Cats/Peer review">Peer review</a> is where editors can put cat-related articles up for a local peer review, which provides scrutiny and suggestions for the further improvement of the article.</li> 
         <li>The <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Cats/Recognized_content" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Cats/Recognized content">Recognized content</a> showcases Featured or Good articles, topics, and pictures, as well as a list of DYKs.</li> 
         <li>The <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Cats/Collaboration" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Cats/Collaboration">Collaboration</a> has been inactive since August 2010. It was established to nominate an article for improvement each month.</li> 
         <li><img alt="Featured article" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/bc/Featured_article_star.svg/16px-Featured_article_star.svg.png" decoding="async" title="Featured article" width="16" height="16" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/bc/Featured_article_star.svg/24px-Featured_article_star.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/bc/Featured_article_star.svg/32px-Featured_article_star.svg.png 2x" data-file-width="180" data-file-height="180"><a href="/wiki/Portal:Cats" title="Portal:Cats">Portal:Cats</a> - Maintain <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Featured_portals#Biology_and_medicine" title="Wikipedia:Featured portals">featured status</a>.</li>
        </ul> 
        <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Activity">Activity</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wikipedia:WikiProject_Cats&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Activity">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
        <p>If you do happen to have some free time on your hands, we would greatly appreciate if you can improve any of the articles which haven't yet reached <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:GA" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:GA">good article</a> status. In almost all cases on Wikipedia, particularly articles which are less than good article status, we can always use additional references and reference citations for any information in any articles. </p>

        <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Recommended_structure">Recommended structure</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wikipedia:WikiProject_Cats&amp;action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: Recommended structure">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
        <p>The currently exists an article entitled <a href="/wiki/List_of_cat_breeds" title="List of cat breeds">List of cat breeds</a>, which enumerates the various recognized breeds. Breed articles that already exist and are labeled with <a href="/wiki/Category:Cat_breeds" title="Category:Cat breeds">Category:Cat breeds</a> appear on that category page, as well. </p>
        <p><i>Title</i>: The article for each breed shall be titled based on the apparently most-common official breed name from the major registries. These names have been placed in the List of cat breeds page. Where the major registries are evenly split on the name, or where the breed is not recognized by the major registries, the article title shall be chosen using the most commonly used name in English language sources. </p>
        <p><i>Redirects</i>: When creating a new page, <a href="/wiki/Redirect" class="mw-disambig" title="Redirect">redirects</a> or <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Disambiguation" title="Wikipedia:Disambiguation">disambiguations</a> under other possible names should be created to point to the page. </p>
        <p>The article on each breed shall include the following information: </p> 
        <ul>
         <li>The <code>{{<a href="/wiki/Template:Infobox_cat_breed" title="Template:Infobox cat breed">Infobox cat breed</a>}}</code></li> 
         <li>Lead section: summary of general information about the breed, brief history, brief description.</li> 
         <li>History: traditional/historical info. modern breed development etc.</li> 
         <li>Characteristics: physical appearance, coat types, colors, temperament etc.</li> 
         <li>Health: known medical issues, etc.</li> 
         <li><i>More?</i></li> 
         <li>See also (optional)</li> 
         <li>References</li> 
         <li>External links (optional): Links to web sites that expand on the encyclopedic info provided by wikipedia. Generally excluded are local breed clubs (too many hundreds to try to maintain the links), commercial sites (where the ads or intent to sell something are blatant), forums and blogs (not particularly encyclopedic).</li>
        </ul>
