                 <div class="description mw-content-ltr en" dir="ltr" lang="en">
                  <span class="language en" title="English"><b>English: </b></span> This is a two-way "translation arrow" icon, drawn by myself in the style of, and modeled after <a href="/wiki/File:Merge-arrow.svg" title="File:Merge-arrow.svg">Merge-arrow.svg</a>. It is meant to more accurately illustrate the process of translating from one regional written language (in this case, a <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Latin_alphabet" class="extiw" title="w:Latin alphabet">Latin</a> '<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/A" class="extiw" title="w:A">A</a>') to another region's written language (a Japanese '<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/A_(kana)" class="extiw" title="w:A (kana)">?</a>' <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hiragana" class="extiw" title="w:Hiragana">Hiragana</a> symbol), and that this process can travel in either direction.
