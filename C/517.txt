    <p class="as-center">Some counterfeit and third party power adapters and batteries may not be designed properly and could result in safety issues. To ensure you receive a genuine Apple battery during a battery replacement, we recommend visiting an <a href="https://www.apple.com/retail/" class="analytics-exitlink">Apple Store</a> or <a href="https://locate.apple.com/" class="analytics-exitlink">Apple Authorized Service Provider</a>. If you need a replacement adapter to charge your Apple device, we recommend getting an Apple power adapter.</p>
