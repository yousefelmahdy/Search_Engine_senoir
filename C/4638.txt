    <h1> GitLab Events </h1> 
    <p> Learn more about software excellence with GitLab. We will be attending, hosting, and running numerous events in 2021, and cannot wait to see you there! </p> 
    <h4> COVID-19 Update </h4> 
    <p> The health and safety of <a href="/handbook/communication/top-misused-terms/">GitLab Team Members</a> and our community are our highest priority. With this in mind we have made the difficult decision to not participate or host any in person corporate events or trade shows till the end of July, 2021. </p> 
    <p> In-person events are a key building block to making our open core and remote work model successful. However, we're committed to team member safety, so we're aiming to postpone as many events as possible or create a virtual version. With community and customer support we will find new creative avenues to strengthen and build deeper bonds during this difficult time. </p> 
    <p> If anyone in the Gitlab Community is being affected and has questions on how to make their new found remote work situation functional and productive please feel free to check out our <a href="/company/culture/all-remote/guide/">guides on remote work</a>. </p>
