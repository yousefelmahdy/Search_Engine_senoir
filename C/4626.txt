       <div class="faq-item"> 
        <div class="question" id="what-are-pipeline-minutes">
          What are pipeline minutes? 
        </div> 
        <div class="answer"> 
         <p>Pipeline minutes are the execution time for your pipelines on our shared runners. Execution on your own runners will not increase your pipeline minutes count and is unlimited.</p> 
        </div> 
       </div> 
       <div class="faq-item"> 
        <div class="question" id="what-happens-if-i-reach-my-minutes-limit">
          What happens if I reach my minutes limit? 
        </div> 
        <div class="answer"> 
         <p>If you reach your limits, you can <a href="https://about.gitlab.com/pricing/faq-consumption-cicd/#managing-your-cicd-minutes-usage">manage your CI/CD minutes usage</a>, <a href="https://customers.gitlab.com/plans">purchase additional CI minutes</a>, or upgrade your account to Premium or Ultimate. Your own runners can still be used even if you reach your limits.</p> 
        </div> 
       </div> 
       <div class="faq-item"> 
        <div class="question" id="does-the-minute-limit-apply-to-all-runners">
          Does the minute limit apply to all runners? 
        </div> 
        <div class="answer"> 
         <p>No. We will only restrict your minutes for our shared runners. If you have a <a href="https://docs.gitlab.com/runner/">specific runner setup for your projects</a>, there is no limit to your build time on GitLab SaaS.</p> 
        </div> 
       </div> 
       <div class="faq-item"> 
        <div class="question" id="do-plans-increase-the-minutes-limit-depending-on-the-number-of-users-in-that-group">
          Do plans increase the minutes limit depending on the number of users in that group? 
        </div> 
        <div class="answer"> 
         <p>No. The limit will be applied to a group, no matter the number of users in that group.</p> 
        </div> 
       </div> 
       <div class="faq-item"> 
        <div class="question" id="why-do-i-need-to-enter-credit-debit-card-details-for-free-pipeline-minutes">
          Why do I need to enter credit/debit card details for free pipeline minutes? 
        </div> 
        <div class="answer"> 
         <p>There has been a massive uptick in abuse of free pipeline minutes available on GitLab.com to mine cryptocurrencies - which creates intermittent performance issues for GitLab.com users. To discourage such abuse, credit/debit card details are required if you choose to use GitLab.com shared runners. Credit/debit card details are not required if you bring your own runner or disable shared runners. When you provide the card, it will be verified with a one-dollar authorization transaction. No charge will be made and no money will transfer. Learn more <a href="https://about.gitlab.com/blog/2021/05/17/prevent-crypto-mining-abuse/">here</a></p> 
        </div>

        </div>
