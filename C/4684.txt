         <h2 id="the-entire-devops-lifecycle">The entire DevOps lifecycle<a href="#the-entire-devops-lifecycle" title="Permalink" class="anchor"></a> </h2> 
         <p>GitLab is the first single application for software development, security, and operations that enables <a href="https://about.gitlab.com/topics/concurrent-devops/">Concurrent DevOps</a>. GitLab makes the software lifecycle faster and radically improves the speed of business. </p>
         <p>GitLab provides solutions for <a href="https://about.gitlab.com/stages-devops-lifecycle/">each of the stages of the DevOps lifecycle</a>. </p>
