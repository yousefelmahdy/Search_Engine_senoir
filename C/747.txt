      <div class="column large-4 large-offset-1 small-12 small-offset-0 heading-column"> 
       <h2 class="typography-headline">One stop for&nbsp;support</h2> 
       <div class="column large-5 small-12"> 
        <figure class="icon-global-repair"></figure> 
        <figcaption class="typography-caption">
         Get two options for repairing your iPod touch: mail-in repair or carry-in repair<sup><a href="#footnote-2" class="footnote">2</a></sup>
        </figcaption> 
       </div> 
      </div> 
      <div class="column large-6  small-12"> 
       <p>Because Apple designs iPod, iOS, and many applications, you get a truly integrated system. And only AppleCare+ provides one-stop service and support from Apple experts, so most issues can be resolved in a single call.<sup><a href="#footnote-3" class="footnote">3</a></sup></p> 
       <ul>
