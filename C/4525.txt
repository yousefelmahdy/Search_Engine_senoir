            <h5 class="headline-5 mt2 mb0">SSL / TLS 1.3 Encryption</h5>
            <div class="markdown-block mb0 flex-grow-2">

            <h5 class="headline-5 mt2 mb0">Cache Configuration</h5>
            <div class="markdown-block mb0 flex-grow-2">

            </noscript>
            <h5 class="headline-5 mt2 mb0">Tiered Caching</h5>
            <div class="markdown-block mb0 flex-grow-2">
             <p class="mb4 body-2 mt2">Origin bandwidth costs are expensive, especially egress costs of caching video content across Cloudflare’s network. Cloudflare’s Tiered Caching minimizes server caching requests and reduces bandwidth costs by having selective and regional Tier 1 data centers request and propagate content across Cloudflare’s network.</p>
            </div><a class="mb0 mb7-ns learn-more" href="https://blog.cloudflare.com/introducing-smarter-tiered-cache-topology-generation/" data-tracking-category="newhome-features-blade" data-tracking-action="click" data-tracking-label="features_learn_more-[https://blog.cloudflare.com/introducing-smarter-tiered-cache-topology-generation/]">Learn more</a>
