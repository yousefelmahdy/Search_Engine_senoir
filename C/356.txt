             <h4 class="c05__title  twtr-type--roman-18  twtr-color--dark-gray-neutral"> What is Twitter? 
              <svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" width="24" height="24" viewbox="0 0 24 24" class="c05__faq-icon  twtr-icon--sm  twtr-color-fill--light-gray-neutral"> <path opacity="0" d="M0 0h24v24H0z" /> <path d="M20.207 7.043c-.39-.39-1.023-.39-1.414 0L12 13.836 5.207 7.043c-.39-.39-1.023-.39-1.414 0s-.39 1.023 0 1.414l7.5 7.5c.195.195.45.293.707.293s.512-.098.707-.293l7.5-7.5c.39-.39.39-1.023 0-1.414z" />

             <div class="c05__faq-answer-copy  twtr-rte">
