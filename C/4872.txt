To add a redirect to this category, place <code>{{<a href="/wiki/Template:Rcat_shell" class="mw-redirect" title="Template:Rcat shell">Rcat shell</a>|{{<a href="/wiki/Template:R_to_project_namespace" title="Template:R to project namespace">R to project namespace</a>}}}}</code> on the <i><b>second new line</b></i> (skip a line) after <code>#REDIRECT&nbsp;[[Target page name]]</code>. For more information follow the links. <i>Never <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Substitution" title="Wikipedia:Substitution">substitute</a></i> redirect template(s), nor place them on <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Soft_redirect" title="Wikipedia:Soft redirect">soft redirects</a>.<br><small>See also the <b><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Template_messages/Redirect_pages" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:Template messages/Redirect pages">complete list of redirect templates</a></b> and the <b><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Redirect/Style_guide" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Redirect/Style guide">redirect style guide</a></b>.</small></td>
