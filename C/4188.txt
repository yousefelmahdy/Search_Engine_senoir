       <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Localization">Localization</span></h2> 
       <p>This template is not intended to be localized. </p>
       <p><small></small> </p> 
       <div class="templatebox-languagedependant"> 
        <p>The template does not generate any localizable text;<br> with + or - always the <i>namespace</i> in English: "Template" is displayed;<br> only in combination with <tt>case=i</tt> or <tt>case=n</tt> the namespace can be translated using the {{<a href="/wiki/Template:I18n/namespace" title="Template:I18n/namespace">LangSwitch</a>}} construct (according to <tt>i18n</tt>). </p> 
       </div>
