          <h3>Twitter Community Guidelines from Bank of America Corporation</h3> 
          <p>Find information on Bank of America Corporation's Twitter community guidelines <a target="_blank" href="http://about.bankofamerica.com/en-us/social-media/twitter-community-guidelines.html">here</a>. We have an active social media presence to help you stay connected with us.<br> <br> Tweets from @MerrillLynch and @ML_Careers are generally one-way communications from Merrill. Follow our Twitter handles to catch the latest information.</p> 
          <h2><span class="border--bottom">Here's how it works:</span></h2> 
          <p>"Following" our Twitter handles is a great way to receive relevant, up-to-date information about Merrill. We will generally only use our handles to send out information and don't have the ability to respond. If you do not wish to receive these tweets, please do not follow <a adhocenable="false" href="https://twitter.com/merrilllynch" target="_blank">@MerrillLynch</a> or <a adhocenable="false" href="https://twitter.com/ML_Careers" target="_blank">@ML_Careers</a>.</p> 
          <h3>&nbsp;</h3> 
          <h3>Here's how to follow us on Twitter:</h3> 
          <p>1. If you are not a Twitter user, sign-up for an account at <a adhocenable="false" href="http://www.twitter.com" target="_blank">www.twitter.com</a></p> 
          <p>2. Log in to your account</p> 
          <p>3. Click on the following link: <a adhocenable="false" href="http://www.twitter.com/merrilllynch">http://www.twitter.com/merrilllynch</a> or <a adhocenable="false" href="http://www.twitter.com/ml_careers" target="_blank">www.twitter.com/ml_careers</a></p> 
          <p>4. Click the "follow" button and you will see tweets with news about our company<br> <br> </p> 
          <h3>That's it! Follow us now.</h3> 
          <p>Our Twitter page is public, which means that anyone can see our tweets. Unless you protect your tweets (something you can do in your Twitter account settings), anyone can see your tweets and your tweets may even show up in search-engine results (like on Google or Yahoo!).<br> <br> When tweeting with Merrill, we encourage everyone to:<br> Use common courtesy and be respectful of others.<br> Tweet your own original content, and avoid content that you know to be fraudulent.<br> Never tweet someone else's copyrighted work, unless you have permission.<br> Never tweet personal, identifying or confidential information.<br> <br> <br> If you are a Merrill Lynch Wealth Management (MLWM) client with a specific question about your Merrill account, you can get help by:<br> </p> 
          <ul> 
           <li>Contacting your MLWM Financial Advisor</li> 
           <li>Emailing us at <a href="mailto:Mlolmail_mlolmail@ml.com">Mlolmail_mlolmail@ml.com</a></li> 
           <li>Calling us at 1.800.MERRILL (637.7455)</li> 
          </ul> 
          <p>&nbsp;</p> 
          <p>Any opinions, views, statements, estimates or projections ("posts") posted on this web page are solely those of the individual author(s). Merrill does not endorse, and shall not be responsible for third-party posts.</p> 
          <p>All of our Twitter handles are managed and monitored by Bank of America Corporation.</p> 
          <p>Those are the basics. Our full policies are below.</p> 
          <h3><br> Important Notices:<br> </h3> 
          <p>Because our Twitter handle is public and anyone with a Twitter account can engage with us, we cannot be responsible for views expressed other than our own.<br> <br> Please note that we may unfollow a user who we feel violates our community policy.</p> 
          <h3><br> Interacting with Us on Twitter<br> </h3> 
          <p>Our Twitter handle is managed and monitored by Bank of America Corporation. We're actively watching what people are saying to and about Merrill Lynch on Twitter to ensure we're receiving and/or offering feedback. However, because of the public nature of the platform, we simply can't respond to or engage on all issues.<br> <br> Never disclose any financial information on Twitter. Merrill will never ask you to disclose your social security number, financial account information, passwords or PINs via Twitter. Twitter is a third party site unaffiliated with Merrill. Merrill is not responsible for the privacy or security policies or practices on Twitter or on any of the third party websites that we may link to. You should review the privacy and security practices of Twitter (found at <a adhocenable="false" target="_blank" href="https://twitter.com/privacy">twitter.com/privacy</a>) and all such third party websites. You can review Merrill Lynch privacy and security practices&nbsp;<a adhocenable="false" href="https://www.ml.com/privacy-and-security-center/privacy-and-security.html">here</a>.<br> <br> As a follower of <a adhocenable="false" href="http://www.twitter.com/merrilllynch" target="_blank">@MerrillLynch</a> you consent to receive news, company information and other tweets from <a adhocenable="false" href="http://www.twitter.com/merrilllynch" target="_blank">@MerrillLynch</a> that we think our followers will find useful. If you do not wish to receive these tweets, please do not follow <a adhocenable="false" href="http://www.twitter.com/merrilllynch" target="_blank">@MerrillLynch</a>.<br> <br> In addition, we discourage clicking on links posted by other Twitter users, as these links may pose risks to your computer or take you to inappropriate sites. Also, we don't accept or consider unsolicited ideas. However, if you do send us ideas that we've solicited, what you submit will be governed by our Idea Submission Policy and Terms.<br> <br> Please don't use Twitter to report phishing or criminal activity. Suspicious emails should be forwarded to <a href="mailto:abuse@bankofamerica.com">abuse@bankofamerica.com</a>. If you would like to make a report, please call 800.432.1000 and a representative can assist you.<br> <br> Please note that when visiting any official Bank of America Corporation Twitter page, you are also subject to the Terms and Conditions of Bank of America Corporation's privacy policy and general terms of use.</p> 
          <h3><br> Links to Third-Party Sites<br> </h3> 
          <p>We may occasionally tweet or retweet links to third-party sites when we think you'll find the information helpful. However, please note that this does not in any way constitute an official endorsement of the individual, site or company.<br> <br> Some linked third party sites may contain content provided by paid sponsors of Merrill, and such sponsors will disclose that relationship on their site. Additionally, those paid sponsors' tweets will contain the term "SPON," indicating the paid nature of the relationship. For all linked third party site content, Merrill has not reviewed the entirety of those sites and therefore does not endorse all other content posted to those sites.</p> 
          <h3><br> Additional Information<br> @ML_Careers (for learning about career opportunities at Merrill Lynch)<br> </h3> 
          <p>Stay up-to-date with featured job opportunities at Merrill by following <a adhocenable="false" href="https://twitter.com/ML_Careers" target="_blank">@ML_Careers</a> on Twitter. Please note that Merrill does not accept applications via Twitter. To apply for a position, please visit <a target="_blank" href="http://careers.bankofamerica.com/">http://careers.bankofamerica.com.</a><br> <br> Please note that Merrill recruits and hires qualified candidates without regard to race, religion, color, sex, sexual orientation, gender, gender identity, age, national origin, ancestry, citizenship, veteran, or disability status, or any factor prohibited by law, and as such affirms in policy and practice to support and promote the concept of equal employment opportunity and affirmative action, in accordance with all applicable federal, state, provincial and municipal laws. We also prohibit discrimination on other bases such as ancestry, medical condition, or marital status under applicable laws. Candidates must possess authorization to work in the United States, as it is not the practice of Merrill to sponsor individuals for work visas.<br> <br> If you do not wish to receive these tweets, please do not follow <a adhocenable="false" href="https://twitter.com/ML_Careers" target="_blank">@ML_Careers.</a></p> 
          <h3><br> LinkedIn Community Guidelines from Bank of America Corporation<br> </h3> 
          <p>Find information on Bank of America Corporation's LinkedIn community guidelines <a target="_blank" href="http://about.bankofamerica.com/en-us/social-media/linkedin-community-guidelines.html">here</a>. We encourage you to visit our company pages today to see how we're connected and follow us for career news and official information about Bank of America and Merrill.</p> 
          <h3><br> Privacy and Security Policies<br> </h3> 
          <p>With respect to content collected through LinkedIn, Merrill follows LinkedIn's privacy policy, which can be accessed <a target="_blank" href="https://www.linkedin.com/legal/privacy-policy">here</a>. Please note that when visiting any Merrill LinkedIn page, when applicable you are also subject to the terms and conditions of Merrill's <a target="_blank" href="https://www.ml.com/privacy-and-security-center/privacy-and-security.html">privacy policy</a> and general terms of use, as well as LinkedIn's terms of service and privacy policy. To read LinkedIn's Terms of Service, please <a adhocenable="false" target="_blank" href="https://www.linkedin.com/legal/user-agreement">click here</a>. To protect your privacy and the privacy of others, please do not include personally identifiable information such as Social Security numbers, account numbers, phone numbers or email addresses in any comment or post. If you do include personally identifiable information in your comment, your comment or post may be deleted.</p>
