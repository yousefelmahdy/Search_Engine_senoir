            <li style="list-style-position: outside;">The Provider may access, on your behalf, information about yourself, your Bank of America relationship, and your accounts at Bank of America.</li> 
            <li style="list-style-position: outside;">You should use caution and ensure that the Provider has appropriate policies and practices to protect the privacy and security of any personal information you provide or to which they are gaining access.</li> 
            <li style="list-style-position: outside;">Use of your personal information by the Provider is governed by your agreement with them, not by Bank of America.</li> 
            <li style="list-style-position: outside;">We are not responsible for the use or disclosure of any personal information accessed by any company or person to whom you provide your username and passcode.</li> 
            <li style="list-style-position: outside;">If you share your Bank of America username and passcode or other information about your accounts with others, we will consider that you have authorized any transaction or action initiated by using the access information you provide.</li> 
            <li style="list-style-position: outside;">If you decide to revoke the authority you have given to a Provider, we strongly recommend that you change your Bank of America passcode to ensure that the Provider cannot continue to access your account.</li> 
            <li style="list-style-position: outside;">You may revoke your consent for certain Providers through the Security Center within Bank of America Online Banking.</li>
