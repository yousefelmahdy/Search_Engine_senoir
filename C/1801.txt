       <h3 id="upgrading-your-personal-accounts-subscription"><a href="#upgrading-your-personal-accounts-subscription">Upgrading your personal account's subscription</a></h3> 
       <p>You can upgrade your personal account from GitHub Free to GitHub Pro to get advanced code review tools on private repositories. For more information, see "<a href="/en/articles/github-s-products">GitHub's products</a>."</p> 
       <ol> 
        <li>In the upper-right corner of any page, click your profile photo, then click <strong>Settings</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/settings/userbar-account-settings.png" alt="Settings icon in the user bar"></span></li> 
        <li>In your user settings sidebar, click <strong>Billing &amp; plans</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/settings/settings-sidebar-billing-plans.png" alt="Billing &amp; plans settings"></span></li> 
        <li>Next to "Current plan", click <strong>Upgrade</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/settings_billing_user_upgrade.png" alt="Upgrade button"></span></li> 
        <li>Under "Pro" on the "Compare plans" page, click <strong>Upgrade to Pro</strong>.</li> 
        <li>Under "Upgrade summary", choose whether you want to pay monthly or yearly. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/choose-monthly-or-yearly-billing.png" alt="Radio buttons with choice of monthly or yearly billing"></span></li> 
        <li>Optionally, to see your plan's details, click <strong>Show details</strong>.</li> 
        <li>Under "Payment method", choose to pay with a credit card or PayPal. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/billing_switch_payments.png" alt="Switch Billing payment methods"></span></li> 
        <li>Complete the payment information form, then click <strong>Submit</strong>.</li> 
        <li>Review the information about the charge and your billing date, then click <strong>Upgrade my account</strong> or <strong>Upgrade my organization's account</strong>.</li> 
       </ol> 
       <h3 id="upgrading-your-organizations-subscription"><a href="#upgrading-your-organizations-subscription">Upgrading your organization's subscription</a></h3> 
       <p>You can upgrade your organization from GitHub Free for an organization to GitHub Team to access advanced collaboration and management tools for teams, or upgrade your organization to GitHub Enterprise Cloud for additional security, compliance, and deployment controls. For more information, see "<a href="/en/articles/github-s-products">GitHub's products</a>."</p> 
       <p>Only organization members with the <a href="/en/articles/permission-levels-for-an-organization"><em>owner</em></a> or <a href="/en/articles/adding-a-billing-manager-to-your-organization"><em>billing manager</em></a> role can access or change billing settings for your organization.</p> 
       <ol> 
        <li>In the top right corner of GitHub, click your profile photo, then click <strong>Your organizations</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/profile/your-organizations.png" alt="Your organizations in the profile menu"></span></li> 
        <li>Click <strong>Settings</strong> next to the organization. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/organizations/settings-button.png" alt="The settings button"></span></li> 
        <li>In your organization's Settings sidebar, click <strong>Billing &amp; plans</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/settings_organization_billing_plans_tab.png" alt="Billing settings"></span></li> 
        <li>Click <strong>Upgrade</strong> next to "Current plan".</li> 
        <li>Under "Compare plans", you can compare the features of each plan. Depending on your current plan and what plan you have chosen, click either <strong>Upgrade to Team</strong> or <strong>Upgrade to Enterprise</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/settings-compare-and-upgrade-plans.png" alt="Compare plans and upgrade"></span></li> 
        <li>Under "Upgrade summary", choose whether you want to pay monthly or yearly. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/choose-monthly-or-yearly-billing.png" alt="Radio buttons with choice of monthly or yearly billing"></span></li> 
        <li>Optionally, to see your plan's details, click <strong>Show details</strong>.</li> 
        <li>Under "Payment method", choose to pay with a credit card or PayPal. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/billing_switch_payments.png" alt="Switch Billing payment methods"></span></li> 
        <li>Complete the payment information form, then click <strong>Submit</strong>.</li> 
        <li>Optionally, if your organization is owned by a business or institution, select <strong>This account is owned by a business</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/organizations/organization-belongs-to.png" alt="Checkbox for organizations that are owned by a business"></span></li> 
        <li>If your organization is owned by a business or institution, type the name of the business or institution. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/organizations/name-of-business.png" alt="Name of business or institution field"></span></li> 
        <li>Review the information about the charge and your billing date, then click <strong>Upgrade my account</strong> or <strong>Upgrade my organization's account</strong>.</li> 
       </ol> 
       <h4 id="next-steps-for-organizations-using-github-enterprise-cloud"><a href="#next-steps-for-organizations-using-github-enterprise-cloud">Next steps for organizations using GitHub Enterprise Cloud</a></h4> 
       <p>If you upgraded your organization to GitHub Enterprise Cloud, you can set up identity and access management for your organization. For more information, see "<a href="/en/organizations/managing-saml-single-sign-on-for-your-organization">Managing SAML single sign-on for your organization</a>."</p> 
       <p>If you'd like to use an enterprise account with GitHub Enterprise Cloud, contact <a href="https://enterprise.github.com/contact">GitHub's Sales team</a>. For more information, see "<a href="/en/articles/about-enterprise-accounts">About enterprise accounts</a>."</p> 
       <h3 id="adding-seats-to-your-organization"><a href="#adding-seats-to-your-organization">Adding seats to your organization</a></h3> 
       <p>If you'd like additional users to have access to your GitHub Team organization's private repositories, you can purchase more seats anytime.</p> 
       <ol> 
        <li>In the top right corner of GitHub, click your profile photo, then click <strong>Your organizations</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/profile/your-organizations.png" alt="Your organizations in the profile menu"></span></li> 
        <li>Click <strong>Settings</strong> next to the organization. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/organizations/settings-button.png" alt="The settings button"></span></li> 
        <li>In your organization's Settings sidebar, click <strong>Billing &amp; plans</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/settings_organization_billing_plans_tab.png" alt="Billing settings"></span></li> 
        <li>Use the <strong>Edit</strong> drop-down and click <strong>Add seats</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/add-seats-dropdown.png" alt="Edit drop-down menu"></span></li> 
        <li>Under "Add seats", type the number of seats you'd like to add. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/add-seats-amount.png" alt="Add seats input"></span></li> 
        <li>Click <strong>Add seats</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/add-seats-button.png" alt="Add seats button"></span></li> 
       </ol> 
       <h3 id="switching-your-organization-from-per-repository-to-per-user-pricing"><a href="#switching-your-organization-from-per-repository-to-per-user-pricing">Switching your organization from per-repository to per-user pricing</a></h3> 
       <p>If your organization is using a legacy per-repository billing plan, you can switch to per-user pricing at any time. You will not be able to switch back to repository pricing once you've upgraded to per-user pricing. For more information, see "<a href="/en/articles/about-per-user-pricing">About per-user pricing</a>."</p> 
       <ol> 
        <li>In the top right corner of GitHub, click your profile photo, then click <strong>Your organizations</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/profile/your-organizations.png" alt="Your organizations in the profile menu"></span></li> 
        <li>Click <strong>Settings</strong> next to the organization. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/organizations/settings-button.png" alt="The settings button"></span></li> 
        <li>In your organization's Settings sidebar, click <strong>Billing &amp; plans</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/settings_organization_billing_plans_tab.png" alt="Billing settings"></span></li> 
        <li>To the right of your plan name, use the <strong>Edit</strong> drop-down menu, and select <strong>Edit plan</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/per-user-upgrade-button.png" alt="Edit drop-down menu"></span></li> 
        <li>To the right of "Advanced tools for teams", click <strong>Upgrade now</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/per-user-upgrade-now-button.png" alt="Upgrade now button"></span></li> 
        <li>Under "Compare plans", you can compare the features of each plan. Depending on your current plan and what plan you have chosen, click either <strong>Upgrade to Team</strong> or <strong>Upgrade to Enterprise</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/settings-compare-and-upgrade-plans.png" alt="Compare plans and upgrade"></span></li> 
        <li>Under "Upgrade summary", choose whether you want to pay monthly or yearly. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/choose-monthly-or-yearly-billing.png" alt="Radio buttons with choice of monthly or yearly billing"></span></li> 
        <li>Optionally, if your organization is owned by a business or institution, select <strong>This account is owned by a business</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/organizations/organization-belongs-to.png" alt="Checkbox for organizations that are owned by a business"></span></li> 
        <li>If your organization is owned by a business or institution, type the name of the business or institution. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/organizations/name-of-business.png" alt="Name of business or institution field"></span></li> 
        <li>Review the information about the charge and your billing date, then click <strong>Upgrade my account</strong> or <strong>Upgrade my organization's account</strong>.</li> 
       </ol> 
       <h3 id="troubleshooting-a-500-error-when-upgrading"><a href="#troubleshooting-a-500-error-when-upgrading">Troubleshooting a 500 error when upgrading</a></h3> 
       <p>If you receive a 500 error when trying to upgrade your account:</p> 
       <ul> 
        <li>Try clearing your browser's cache, then resubmit your information</li> 
        <li>Check to make sure you have entered your payment data correctly</li> 
       </ul> 
       <p>If that doesn't resolve the error, please contact <a href="https://support.github.com/contact">GitHub Support</a> or <a href="https://premium.githubsupport.com">GitHub Premium Support</a>.</p>
