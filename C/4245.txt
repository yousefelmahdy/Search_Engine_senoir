         <p align="center"><a href="#LabelingResources">Labeling Resources by Commodity </a>• <a href="#general">General Resources </a>• <a href="#PublicGuidance">Laws, Regulations, and Public Guidance</a> • <a href="#mandatory">Mandatory Labeling Requirements </a>• <a href="#colas"> COLAs Guidance</a> • <a href="#marketing"> Market-based Sampling Program</a></p>

         <p class="DarkBlueBK">Labeling Resources by Commodity</p> 
         <p><strong><a title="Beer Labeling" href="/beer/labeling-and-formulation">Beer Labeling</a></strong></p> 
         <p><strong><a title="Distilled Spirits Labeling" href="/distilled-spirits/labeling">Distilled Spirits Labeling</a></strong></p>

         <p class="DarkBlueBK">General Resources</p> 
         <p>These resources relate to an assortment of labeling topics including processing times for applications, personalized labels, allowable changes, organic claims, and more.</p> 
         <p><a href="/labeling"><strong>Labeling Home Page</strong></a><br>Browse TTB's labeling resources for wine, malt beverages, beer, and distilled spirits, including guidance, statistics, and news and events</p> 
         <p><a href="/labeling/processing-times"><strong>Processing Times for Label Applications</strong></a><br>See how many days it takes to process your application</p> 
         <p><a href="/images/pdfs/naag.pdf" target="_blank"><strong>Prevention of Misleading Health-related Statements<br></strong></a>TTB's response to the National Association of Attorney Generals concerns on the formulation and marketing of alcohol beverages that contain ingredients typically associated with non-alcohol energy drinks.</p> 
         <p><a title="Allowable Changes to Approved Labels" href="/allowable-revisions/allowable-changes-sample-label-generator"><strong>Allowable Changes to Approved Labels</strong></a><br>You can make certain changes to your labels <em>without </em>obtaining a new certificate of label approval or COLA (<a href="/images/pdfs/forms/f510031.pdf" target="_blank">TTB Form 5100.31</a>). Look at some sample changes to labels, guidance information, and the complete list of allowable revisions.</p> 
         <p><a href="/images/pdfs/g20115-personalized-labels.pdf" target="_blank"><strong>Personalized Labels<br></strong></a>TTB provides the beverage alcohol industry with clear guidance on our long standing policies as it relates to personalized labeling.</p> 
         <p><strong>What You Should Know About Labeling<br></strong>These TTB brochures provide explanations of the information that must be provided to the public on the following labels:</p> 
         <ul> 
          <li><a href="https://www.ttb.gov/images/pdfs/p51901.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Wine Labels</a></li> 
          <li><a href="/images/pdfs/p51903.pdf" target="_blank">Malt Beverage Labels</a></li> 
          <li><a href="/images/pdfs/p51902.pdf" target="_blank">Distilled Spirits Labels</a></li> 
         </ul> 
         <p><a href="/appellations-of-origin"><strong>U.S. Appellations of Origin</strong></a><br> Learn all about appellations of origin—approved U.S. viticultural areas as identified in 27 CFR part 9—at TTB's "U.S. Appellations of Origins" page. Learn about foreign appellations of origin, as well.</p> 
         <p><a title="Alcohol Beverages Labeled with Organic Claims" href="/alfd/alcohol-beverages-labeled-with-organic-claims"><strong>Labels with Organic Claims</strong></a><br> TTB provides you with the most up-to-date information for the production, handling, processing, labeling, and marketing of products labeled with organic claims.</p> 
         <ul> 
          <li><a href="/images/pdfs/mou-ams.pdf" target="_blank">MOU Between TTB and the Agricultural Marketing Service</a></li> 
         </ul> 
         <p><a title="Alcohol Fact Labeling Methods" href="/scientific-services-division/alcohol-fact-labeling-methods"><strong>Allergen Labeling</strong></a></p> 
         <p>TTB periodically reviews labeling and advertising claims by taking samples of alcohol beverage products for validation purposes.&nbsp; TTB's <a title="Alcohol Fact Labeling Methods" href="/scientific-services-division/alcohol-fact-labeling-methods">Alcohol Fact Labeling Methods</a> page provides details on this process.</p> 
         <p><a title="Alcohol Fact Labeling Methods" href="/faqs/alcohol-beverages-with-added-caffiene"><strong>Alcohol Beverages With Added Caffeine</strong></a><br>TTB periodically reviews labeling and advertising claims by taking samples of alcohol beverage products for validation purposes. TTB's <a title="Alcohol Fact Labeling Methods" href="/scientific-services-division/alcohol-fact-labeling-methods">Alcohol Fact Labeling Methods</a> page provides details on this process.</p> 
         <p><a title="Alcohol Fact Labeling Methods" href="/scientific-services-division/alcohol-fact-labeling-methods"><strong>Labeling Fact Review Methods</strong></a><br>TTB periodically reviews labeling and advertising claims by taking samples of alcohol beverage products for validation purposes. TTB's <a title="Alcohol Fact Labeling Methods" href="/scientific-services-division/alcohol-fact-labeling-methods">Alcohol Fact Labeling Methods</a> page provides details on this process.</p> 
         <p><a title="Formula Approval Basics for Domestic Producers and Importers of Alcohol Beverages" href="/formulation/approval-basics"><strong>Pre-COLA Evaluation</strong></a><br>For many alcohol beverage products, TTB requires a product evaluation to determine whether a proposed label identifies the product in an adequate and non-misleading way. Visit TTB's <a href="/formulation">Formulation</a> pages to learn more.</p> 
         <p><a id="PublicGuidance" name="PublicGuidance"></a></p> 
         <p class="DarkBlueBK">Laws, Regulations, and Public Guidance</p> 
         <p>Laws and regulations set out the requirements for alcohol beverage labeling. Public guidance includes rulings, procedures, industry circulars, and other publications issued to help the regulated industries understand TTB regulations, policies, and other requirements as it relates to labeling.</p> 
         <p><a title="Labeling Laws and Regulations" href="/labeling/laws-and-regulations"><strong>Laws and Regulations</strong></a><br>TTB's labeling laws and regulations</p> 
         <p><a href="/laws-regulations-and-public-guidance/labeling-act-penalty"><strong>Penalty for Violations of the Alcoholic Beverage Labeling Act</strong></a><br>See the penalty for violating this Act, which requires a health warning statement on the labels of all containers of alcoholic beverages sold or distributed in the United States.</p> 
         <p><a title="Alcohol Labeling Rulings" href="/labeling/labeling-rulings"><strong>Rulings</strong></a><br>TTB's labeling rulings</p> 
         <p><a title="labeling industry circulars" href="/labeling/industry-circulars"><strong>Industry Circulars</strong></a><br>Keep up-to-date with information concerning TTB-regulated industry.</p> 
         <ul> 
          <li><a href="/images/industry_circulars/archives/2014/14-02.html" target="_blank">2014-2 - Expansion of
