        <p>Instructure is an education technology company dedicated to improving digital teaching and learning experiences. We believe in the power of people to grow and succeed throughout their lives, and our goal is to amplify that power. Our lifelong learning platform connects all the digital tools, services, and content that educators and learners love.&nbsp;It includes solutions for learning management, video learning, assessment, cataloging, career readiness, and more.</p>
