           <h1> <span>Bank of America CEO to Participate at BofA Securities Future of Financials 2020 Virtual Conference </span> </h1> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
         <div class="row"> 
          <div class="col-lg-9"> 
           <div class="pub-date"> <span> November 2, 2020 at 10:00 AM Eastern </span> 
           </div> 
           <div> 
            <div class="richtext text"> 
             <div class="cmp-text"> 
              <p>Bank of America Chairman and Chief Executive Officer Brian Moynihan will participate in the BofA Securities Future of Financials 2020 Virtual Conference on Monday, November 9 at 8 a.m. ET. A live audio webcast of this virtual event will be accessible through the Bank of America Investor Relations website at&nbsp;<u><a href="http://investor.bankofamerica.com/" target="_blank" title="Investor Relations website">http://investor.bankofamerica.com</a></u>. A replay will also be available.<br> &nbsp;</p> 
              <h5>Bank of America</h5> 
              <p>Bank of America is one of the world’s leading financial institutions, serving individual consumers, small and middle-market businesses and large corporations with a full range of banking, investing, asset management and other financial and risk management products and services. The company provides unmatched convenience in the United States, serving approximately 66 million consumer and small business clients with approximately 4,300 retail financial centers, including approximately 2,900 lending centers, 2,500 financial centers with a Consumer Investment Financial Solutions Advisor and approximately 2,300 business centers; approximately 17,000 ATMs; and award-winning digital banking with approximately 39 million active users, including approximately 31 million mobile users. Bank of America is a global leader in wealth management, corporate and investment banking and trading across a broad range of asset classes, serving corporations, governments, institutions and individuals around the world. Bank of America offers industry-leading support to approximately 3 million small business owners through a suite of innovative, easy-to-use online products and services. The company serves clients through operations across the United States, its territories and approximately 35 countries. Bank of America Corporation stock (NYSE: BAC) is listed on the New York Stock Exchange.</p> 
              <p>For more Bank of America news, including dividend announcements and other important information, visit the&nbsp;<u><a href="https://newsroom.bankofamerica.com/" target="_blank" title="Bank of America newsroom">Bank of America newsroom</a></u>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<u><a href="https://newsroom.bankofamerica.com/alerts" target="_blank" title="Register for press release alerts">register for email news alerts</a>.</u><br> </p> 
              <p style="text-align: center;"><u><b><a href="http://www.bankofamerica.com/" target="_blank" title="Bank of America website">www.bankofamerica.com</a></b></u></p> 
              <p style="text-align: center;">###</p>

              <p>Jonathan G. Blum, Bank of America (Fixed Income)&nbsp;<br> Phone: 1.212.449.3112<br> <u><a href="mailto:jonathan.blum@bofa.com" title="Email Jonathan Blum">jonathan.blum@bofa.com</a></u></p>
