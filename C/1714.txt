       <h1 class="title"> What We Do </h1> 
       <div class="uf-meta-data"> 
        <div itemprop="author" itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">

       <div id="uf-item-blog-content" class="uf-item-blog-content"> 
        <p>Instructure provides a rich ecosystem of digital teaching and learning solutions that help make 21st-century education a simpler and more positive experience. We're constantly refining and growing our platform to support and inspire educators and learners as education evolves.</p>
        <p><a class="content-button red" href="https://www.instructure.com/canvas/resources/about-us/who-we-are">Who we are</a><a class="content-button white" href="https://www.instructure.com/contact-us">Contact Us</a></p>
        <div class="ob-video-container">
         <iframe allowfullscreen allowtransparency="true" class="wistia_embed" frameborder="0" name="wistia_embed" scrolling="no" src="https://fast.wistia.net/embed/iframe/imsf35e03l" title="Wistia video player"></iframe>
        </div>
        <h2>Our Offerings</h2>
        <p>Education is a lifelong endeavor. Each stage has distinct goals and specific teaching and learning needs. Our products, tools, and services are tailored to meet the unique requirements of K–12, higher education, and business.</p>
        <div class="ob-content-3-col-container"> 
         <div class="ob-content-column">
          <img alt="Canvas K-12 image" class="ob-content-about-image" src="https://content.cdntwrk.com/files/aHViPTExOTc0MyZjbWQ9aXRlbWVkaXRvcmltYWdlJmZpbGVuYW1lPWl0ZW1lZGl0b3JpbWFnZV82MDYyMjE1ZjlhZTIzLnBuZyZ2ZXJzaW9uPTAwMDAmc2lnPTM3ZDQ2YzM0MmY0NDQ1ODBmZGJiYWNhNzRiM2Q4YTk1"> 
          <h3>K–12</h3> 
          <p>User-friendly tools for day-to-day teaching and learning, student-centered assessment, and effective professional development.</p> 
          <p><a class="ob-content-link" href="https://www.instructure.com/canvas/k-12">Learn more</a></p> 
         </div> 
         <div class="ob-content-column">
          <img alt="Canvas higher education image" class="ob-content-about-image" src="https://content.cdntwrk.com/files/aHViPTExOTc0MyZjbWQ9aXRlbWVkaXRvcmltYWdlJmZpbGVuYW1lPWl0ZW1lZGl0b3JpbWFnZV82MDYyMjFiZTJjODhhLnBuZyZ2ZXJzaW9uPTAwMDAmc2lnPTA2ZTBkYjM2NzRmZDJlYzhmN2YwYmM1Y2JkZjNiOTVh"> 
          <h3>Higher Ed</h3> 
          <p>A modern education experience for modern students—easy course and program management, improved student engagement, connections to career readiness, and more.</p> 
          <p><a class="ob-content-link" href="https://www.instructure.com/canvas/higher-education">Learn more</a></p> 
         </div> 
         <div class="ob-content-column">
          <img alt="Canvas business image" class="ob-content-about-image" src="https://content.cdntwrk.com/files/aHViPTExOTc0MyZjbWQ9aXRlbWVkaXRvcmltYWdlJmZpbGVuYW1lPWl0ZW1lZGl0b3JpbWFnZV82MDYyMjIzOTIwNmM4LnBuZyZ2ZXJzaW9uPTAwMDAmc2lnPWMzZTllY2EzODMyZDUzNTA0Nzc2NTZhYjk3ZmE3NDli"> 
          <h3>Business</h3> 
          <p>A platform to help Fortune 1000 companies create a culture of continuous learning and development. Reach every employee, regardless of role or location, and give them the tools to succeed.</p> 
          <p><a class="ob-content-link" href="https://www.instructure.com/corporate-canvas">Learn more</a></p>
