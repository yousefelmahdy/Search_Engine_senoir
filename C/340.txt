      <div class="lpc-faqs-faq--answer lp-faqs-faq--answer no-widows lp-text">

The annual and biannual plans come included with a free domain for one year. Domains registered elsewhere can be used on WordPress.com using <a href="https://wordpress.com/support/domains/map-existing-domain/" target="_blank">Domain Mapping</a> or <a href="https://wordpress.com/support/incoming-domain-transfer/" target="_blank">transferred to us</a> to manage everything in one place.
