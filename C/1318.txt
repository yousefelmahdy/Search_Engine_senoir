        <section class="resource-details"> 
         <div class="resource-details__inner"> 
          <h2 class="subpage__subheading">Using the YouTube Logo</h2> 
          <div class="resource-detail"> 
           <div class="resource-detail__description"> 
            <div class="resource-detail__description-text"> 
             <h3 class="resource-detail__description-title">Clear Space</h3> 
             <p class="resource-detail__description-copy">Clear space buffers the logo from images, text, or other graphics that compromise its impact and visibility. The more breathing room you give our logo, the greater the impact it can have.</p> 
             <p class="resource-detail__description-copy">The amount of clear space around the logo should be equal to or greater than the height of the icon.</p> 
            </div> 
           </div> 
           <div class="resource-detail__visuals"> 
            <div class="resource-detail__visual-asset"> 
             <img class="resource-detail__visual-asset-image" src="https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/92t2tkjNp2Sjtrt9L0xCqRIYguMPraVJEc85TcLPc30eI_dUSa0dpCpOPOwmzBqD0HVzwXpta92FbLa4Ur-ZlTk1qLaPuYJpoTx5qg" alt="YouTube logo, full color, on a light backround"> 
            </div> 
           </div> 
          </div> 
          <div class="resource-detail"> 
           <div class="resource-detail__description"> 
            <div class="resource-detail__description-text"> 
             <h3 class="resource-detail__description-title">Sizing</h3> 
             <p class="resource-detail__description-copy">We’ve optimized our logo for specific sizes. Our logo needs to be crystal clear and legible, whether it appears on a smartphone or an arena’s jumbo screen.</p> 
            </div> 
           </div> 
           <div class="resource-detail__visuals"> 
            <div class="resource-detail__visual-asset"> 
             <img class="resource-detail__visual-asset-image" src="https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/zGEp7ZcdVPAYpoFeysw-HTxmA2ROTBBZSyI_Fi-CRe0wGBf882Ak8LyV29415XFeWtaK0gPSyuNA8R6sK4o8k1QfcppZF1xiA8BA6Q" alt="YouTube logo, full color, on a light backround" style="width: auto; max-width: none; height: 30px;"> 
             <p class="resource-detail__visual-asset-description">Minimum digital height: <b>20dp</b></p> 
            </div> 
            <div class="resource-detail__visual-asset"> 
             <img class="resource-detail__visual-asset-image" src="https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/gDZd7QoeoCFDUv8Mgro2veo7Ls8rzd9KvyNCoxfEIlTi7a3AIF3A6ItmUISJ6w1hbZlq4uN3Hei6dC1GJ_rZwAUrhmPt0afsvz9fHg" alt="YouTube logo, full color, on a light backround" style="width: auto; max-width: none; height: 24px;"> 
             <p class="resource-detail__visual-asset-description">Minimum print height: <b>0.125in or 3.1mm</b></p> 
            </div> 
           </div> 
          </div> 
          <div class="resource-detail"> 
           <div class="resource-detail__description"> 
            <div class="resource-detail__description-text"> 
             <h3 class="resource-detail__description-title">What not to do with the Logo</h3> 
             <p class="resource-detail__description-copy">The YouTube Logo is a symbol people recognize, so it should never be altered.</p> 
             <p class="resource-detail__description-copy">Here are a few examples of what not to do with the YouTube Logo.</p> 
             <h4 class="resource-detail__list-title resource-detail__list-title--dont"> <img src="https://kstatic.googleusercontent.com/files/d50cd459308ad47b7f98567963cb67eefff6f895d83ead793530f3a63261379c6e00cae6e7970a98a647c97de6f63639fde45ccf065740f7d5da3967b77947be" alt="Don't">Don't</h4> 
             <ul class="resource-detail__list"> 
              <li class="resource-detail__list-item">Change the spacing between the icon and the word "YouTube" or its letters</li> 
              <li class="resource-detail__list-item">Use any colors other than red, almost black, or white</li> 
              <li class="resource-detail__list-item">Choose a different typeface for "YouTube"</li> 
              <li class="resource-detail__list-item">Add visual effects like a drop shadow</li> 
              <li class="resource-detail__list-item">Change or replace the word "YouTube" in any way</li> 
              <li class="resource-detail__list-item">Change the shape of the Logo</li> 
              <li class="resource-detail__list-item">Use the Logo in a phrase or sentence</li> 
             </ul> 
            </div> 
           </div> 
           <div class="resource-detail__visuals"> 
            <div class="resource-detail__visual-asset"> 
             <img class="resource-detail__visual-asset-image" src="https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/N1D-bPT50Q7W2pnmhiH1pVhNwlQEM4x9SJVtYv61HnODNfD09uks1wYg0-07VwHuGEiE8rH4F_5rPvxSIozKp3XY4KZRAHq63IZQmg" alt="Common mistakes when implementing YouTube logos"> 
            </div> 
           </div> 
          </div> 
          <div class="resource-detail"> 
           <div class="resource-detail__description"> 
            <div class="resource-detail__description-text"> 
             <h3 class="resource-detail__description-title">Using the Logo on solid backgrounds</h3> 
             <p class="resource-detail__description-copy">These examples show the correct application of the YouTube Logo on different solid backgrounds. The almost black full-color Logo should be used on a background that’s lighter than 40% gray. The white full-color Logo should be used on a background that’s darker than 50% gray.</p> 
            </div> 
           </div> 
           <div class="resource-detail__visuals"> 
            <div class="resource-detail__visual-asset"> 
             <img class="resource-detail__visual-asset-image" src="https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/cOKUOqMLCbZu6y4_J0HvSFylHiFwZVpTqN0_wYOeT3Dms5825uIc_Vz1il3rsLUdLaeIgWGiYlkXOpkqROt_GsBpcsvmI2eleTxa" alt="YouTube logo on solid backgrounds"> 
            </div> 
           </div> 
          </div> 
          <div class="resource-detail"> 
           <div class="resource-detail__description"> 
            <div class="resource-detail__description-text"> 
             <h3 class="resource-detail__description-title">Full-color Logo</h3> 
             <p class="resource-detail__description-copy">There are two versions of the full-color Logo, almost black and white – but the triangle in the Icon should always be white.</p> 
             <p class="resource-detail__description-copy">Use the almost black full-color Logo on a light background. Use the white full-color Logo on a dark background.</p> 
            </div> 
           </div>
