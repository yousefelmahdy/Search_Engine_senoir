      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Logging_a_transwiki_of_a_page">Logging a transwiki of a page</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wikipedia:Transwiki_log&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Logging a transwiki of a page">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>Transwiki is usually an automated process for most of the Wikipedia's sister projects, so entries do not need to be manually logged. </p>
      <p>For information about manual transwiki-ing marked articles and listing them here, see <a href="https://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/Transwiki" class="extiw" title="m:Transwiki">m:Transwiki</a>. If you are completing a transwiki, log which page was moved, and the address it moved to. Only in this case you should sign your name using ~~~~. If a page listed for transfer was deleted, please record the reason why. </p> 
      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Instructions">Instructions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wikipedia:Transwiki_log&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Instructions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>After a transwiki, there are three possible routes for an article. After you have checked any of the following articles and ensured one of these steps has been taken, be sure to mark it by <s>striking through</s> the listing with <code>&lt;s&gt; &lt;/s&gt;</code> tags and note how it was resolved. There is no voting involved; simply strike out the entry for whatever valid reason, note that reason, and your judgement is enough. When doing this <i>please do not sign</i> your edits, as this is unneeded and the page is long enough as it is. For example: </p> 
      <ul>
       <li><s><a href="/w/index.php?title=Eurasian_Smore&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Eurasian Smore (page does not exist)">Eurasian Smore</a> </s>? <a href="https://en.wikibooks.org/wiki/Transwiki:Eurasian_Smore" class="extiw" title="wikibooks:Transwiki:Eurasian Smore">Wikibooks:Transwiki:Eurasian Smore</a>. (Deleted)</li>
      </ul> 
      <p>Any red links (deleted articles) should always be crossed out as the fact that they do not exist is a proof that they have been successfully dealt with. </p>
      <p>Crossed-out articles are eventually added to a permanent archives, as a record. </p> 
      <h3><span id=".281..29_Create_a_redirect"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="(1.)_Create_a_redirect">(1.) Create a redirect</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wikipedia:Transwiki_log&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: (1.) Create a redirect">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3> 
      <p><i>See <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Redirect" title="Wikipedia:Redirect">Wikipedia:Redirect</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Merging" title="Wikipedia:Merging">Wikipedia:Merging</a></i> </p>
      <p>If the information in the article would be more appropriate as part of another article, you can <b>merge</b> and <b>redirect</b>. If the information in the article already exists in another article, or if the title of the article could reasonably be considered a useable term by which a user would search for information, you can simply create a <b>redirect</b>. If the topic is overly specific, and therefore has very little potential for growth, it is preferable to redirect the transwikied article to a more general article. </p> 
      <h3><span id=".282..29_Expand_the_article"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="(2.)_Expand_the_article">(2.) Expand the article</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wikipedia:Transwiki_log&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: (2.) Expand the article">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3> 
      <p><i>See <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:How_to_edit_a_page" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:How to edit a page">Wikipedia:How to edit a page</a></i> </p>
      <p>Of course if the article has an obvious potential for encyclopedic growth, and has a conventional name, then you can help expanding it! You may keep a link to a transwikied article, preferably via a template such as {{<a href="/wiki/Template:Wiktionary" title="Template:Wiktionary">wiktionary</a>}} or similar. </p> 
      <h3><span id=".283..29_Delete_the_article"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="(3.)_Delete_the_article">(3.) Delete the article</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wikipedia:Transwiki_log&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: (3.) Delete the article">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3> 
      <p>If the transwikied article is unencyclopedic and neither (1) or (2) apply, it may be submitted for deletion. </p> 
      <ul>
       <li>If an article has already been discussed on <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Articles_for_deletion" title="Wikipedia:Articles for deletion">Wikipedia:Articles for deletion</a> (AfD), and the outcome was to transwiki, then it doesn't have to be discussed again, simply flag it for speedy deletion using {{<a href="/wiki/Template:Db-a5" title="Template:Db-a5">db-a5</a>}} template.</li>
      </ul> 
      <ul>
       <li>Otherwise, you have a choice to either: 
        <ul>
         <li>submit it to AfD process <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:BEFORE" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:BEFORE">as described here</a>,</li> 
         <li>submit it to <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Proposed_deletion" title="Wikipedia:Proposed deletion">proposed deletion</a> (Prod) process via {{subst:<a href="/wiki/Template:Prod" class="mw-redirect" title="Template:Prod">prod</a>}} template.</li>
        </ul></li>
      </ul> 
      <ul>
       <li>Follow the appropriate deletion procedure. <b>Wait</b> for its end, <b>then strike out</b> an entry and log the result.</li>
      </ul> 
      <ul>
       <li>If the result is "delete", you may instead leave the boilerplate text in place of the original article:</li>
      </ul> 
      <dl>
       <dd>
        <dl>
         <dd>
          <tt>''The contents of this page and the page's history have been moved to [[interwiki:Transwiki:Article Name]] via the [[m:transwiki|transwiki]] system, all future edits should go there.'' ~~~~</tt>
         </dd>
        </dl>
       </dd>
      </dl> 
      <ul>
       <li>
        <ul>
         <li>There are also specialized boilerplate templates, such as {{<a href="/wiki/Template:Wi" class="mw-redirect" title="Template:Wi">wi</a>}} for Wiktionary.</li>
        </ul></li>
      </ul>
