       <h3 id="requirements"><a href="#requirements">Requirements</a></h3> 
       <p>Before you start, make sure you know:</p> 
       <ul> 
        <li>The GitHub username of the client who will become the owner of the organization you create</li> 
        <li>The name your client would like to use for the organization</li> 
        <li>The email address where you would like receipts to be sent</li> 
        <li>The <a href="/en/articles/github-s-products">product</a> your client would like to purchase</li> 
        <li>The number of <a href="/en/articles/about-per-user-pricing">paid seats</a> your client would like you to purchase for the organization</li> 
       </ul> 
       <h3 id="step-1-create-your-personal-github-account"><a href="#step-1-create-your-personal-github-account">Step 1: Create your personal GitHub account</a></h3> 
       <p>You will use your personal account to set up the organization. You'll also need to sign in to this account to renew or make changes to your client's subscription in the future.</p> 
       <p>If you already have a personal GitHub user account, skip to <a href="#step-2-create-the-organization">step 2</a>.</p> 
       <ol> 
        <li>Go to the <a href="https://github.com/join">Join GitHub</a> page.</li> 
        <li>Under "Create your personal account," type your username, email address, and password, then click <strong>Create an account</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/billing_create_your_personal_account_form.png" alt="Create personal account entry form"></span></li> 
        <li>Select GitHub Free for your personal account.</li> 
        <li>Click <strong>Finish sign up</strong>.</li> 
       </ol> 
       <h3 id="step-2-create-the-organization"><a href="#step-2-create-the-organization">Step 2: Create the organization</a></h3> 
       <ol> 
        <li>In the upper-right corner of any page, click your profile photo, then click <strong>Settings</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/settings/userbar-account-settings.png" alt="Settings icon in the user bar"></span></li> 
        <li>In your user settings sidebar, click <strong>Organizations</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/settings/settings-user-orgs.png" alt="User settings for organizations"></span></li> 
        <li>In the "Organizations" section, click <strong>New organization</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/settings/new-org-button.png" alt="Button for creating a new organization"></span></li> 
        <li>Under "Choose a plan", click <strong>Choose GitHub Free</strong>. You will upgrade the organization in the next step.</li> 
        <li>Under "Organization account name", type a name for your organization account. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/organizations/new-org-name.png" alt="Field to type an organization name"></span></li> 
        <li>Under "Contact email", type a contact email address for your client. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/organizations/contact-email-field.png" alt="Contact email field"></span></li> 
        <li>Optionally, if your organization is owned by a business or institution, select <strong>This account is owned by a business</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/organizations/organization-belongs-to.png" alt="Checkbox for organizations that are owned by a business"></span></li> 
        <li>If your organization is owned by a business or institution, type the name of the business or institution. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/organizations/name-of-business.png" alt="Name of business or institution field"></span></li> 
        <li>Click <strong>Next</strong>.</li> 
       </ol> 
       <h3 id="step-3-upgrade-the-organization-to-a-yearly-paid-subscription"><a href="#step-3-upgrade-the-organization-to-a-yearly-paid-subscription">Step 3: Upgrade the organization to a yearly paid subscription</a></h3> 
       <ol> 
        <li>In the top right corner of GitHub, click your profile photo, then click <strong>Your organizations</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/profile/your-organizations.png" alt="Your organizations in the profile menu"></span></li> 
        <li>Click <strong>Settings</strong> next to the organization. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/organizations/settings-button.png" alt="The settings button"></span></li> 
        <li>In your organization's Settings sidebar, click <strong>Billing &amp; plans</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/settings_organization_billing_plans_tab.png" alt="Billing settings"></span></li> 
        <li>Click <strong>Upgrade</strong> next to "Current plan".</li> 
        <li>Under "Compare plans", you can compare the features of each plan. Depending on your current plan and what plan you have chosen, click either <strong>Upgrade to Team</strong> or <strong>Upgrade to Enterprise</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/settings-compare-and-upgrade-plans.png" alt="Compare plans and upgrade"></span> (You can add more seats to the organization in the next step.)</li> 
        <li>Under "Upgrade summary", select <strong>Pay yearly</strong> to pay for the organization yearly. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/choose-annual-billing-org-resellers.png" alt="Radio button for yearly billing"></span></li> 
        <li>Under "Payment method", choose to pay with a credit card or PayPal. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/billing_switch_payments.png" alt="Switch Billing payment methods"></span></li> 
        <li>Complete the payment information form, then click <strong>Submit</strong>.</li> 
        <li>Review the information about the charge and your billing date, then click <strong>Upgrade my account</strong> or <strong>Upgrade my organization's account</strong>.</li> 
       </ol> 
       <h3 id="step-4-upgrade-the-number-of-paid-seats-in-the-organization"><a href="#step-4-upgrade-the-number-of-paid-seats-in-the-organization">Step 4: Upgrade the number of paid seats in the organization</a></h3> 
       <ol> 
        <li>In the top right corner of GitHub, click your profile photo, then click <strong>Your organizations</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/profile/your-organizations.png" alt="Your organizations in the profile menu"></span></li> 
        <li>Click <strong>Settings</strong> next to the organization. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/organizations/settings-button.png" alt="The settings button"></span></li> 
        <li>In your organization's Settings sidebar, click <strong>Billing &amp; plans</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/settings_organization_billing_plans_tab.png" alt="Billing settings"></span></li> 
        <li>Use the <strong>Edit</strong> drop-down and click <strong>Add seats</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/add-seats-dropdown.png" alt="Edit drop-down menu"></span></li> 
        <li>Under "Add seats", type the number of seats you'd like to add. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/add-seats-amount.png" alt="Add seats input"></span></li> 
        <li>Click <strong>Add seats</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/billing/add-seats-button.png" alt="Add seats button"></span></li> 
       </ol>
