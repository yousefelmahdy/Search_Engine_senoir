       <h3 id="joining-github-sponsors"><a href="#joining-github-sponsors">Joining GitHub Sponsors</a></h3> 
       <p>Any organization that contributes to an open source project and legally operates in a supported region is eligible to become a sponsored organization. If your organization operates in a region that isn't already supported by GitHub Sponsors, join the waitlist at <a href="https://github.com/sponsors">GitHub Sponsors</a>. For a list of supported regions, see <a href="https://github.com/sponsors#countries">GitHub Sponsors</a>.</p> 
       <p>After you receive an invitation for your organization to join GitHub Sponsors, you can complete the steps below to become a sponsored organization.</p> 
       <p>To join GitHub Sponsors as an individual contributor outside an organization, see "<a href="/en/sponsors/receiving-sponsorships-through-github-sponsors/setting-up-github-sponsors-for-your-user-account">Setting up GitHub Sponsors for your user account</a>."</p> 
       <ol> 
        <li>Navigate to <a href="https://github.com/sponsors">GitHub Sponsors</a>.</li> 
        <li>Click <strong>View your eligible accounts</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/view-eligible-accounts.png" alt="View your eligible accounts button"></span></li> 
        <li>To the right of your organization, click <strong>Join the waitlist</strong>.</li> 
        <li>Follow the prompts to complete your contact information.</li> 
        <li>Review the <a href="/en/github/site-policy/github-sponsors-additional-terms">GitHub Sponsors Additional Terms</a> and <a href="/en/articles/github-privacy-statement">GitHub Privacy Statement</a>, then click <strong>Join the waitlist</strong>.</li> 
       </ol> 
       <h3 id="completing-your-sponsored-organization-profile"><a href="#completing-your-sponsored-organization-profile">Completing your sponsored organization profile</a></h3> 
       <ol> 
        <li>In the upper-right corner of any page, click your profile photo, then click <strong>Your sponsors</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/access-github-sponsors-dashboard.png" alt="GitHub Sponsors button"></span></li> 
        <li>If a list of your sponsored and eligible accounts is shown, to the right of the account you want to manage, click <strong>Dashboard</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/dev-sponsors-dashboard-button.png" alt="Developer sponsors dashboard button"></span></li> 
        <li>On the left sidebar, click <strong>Profile details</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/profile-tab.png" alt="Profile details tab"></span></li> 
        <li>Under "Short bio", type a brief description of yourself and your work. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/short-bio.png" alt="Short bio field"></span></li> 
        <li>Under "Introduction", type a description of the work you'll do with your sponsorships. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/introduction-box.png" alt="Profile details text field"></span></li> 
        <li>Optionally, choose the organization members that are highlighted on your GitHub Sponsors profile.
         <ul> 
          <li>To the right of "Meet the team", click <strong>Edit</strong>.<br> <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/edit-org-members-profile-button.png" alt="Edit organization members to profile button"></span></li> 
          <li>Select up to six organization members, then click <strong>Save</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/select-highlighted-org-members.png" alt="Select highlighted organization members"></span></li> 
          <li>Optionally, under each highlighted member's name, type a description of the member. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/description-highlighted-org-members.png" alt="Description for highlighted organization members"></span></li> 
         </ul></li> 
        <li>Optionally, choose the repositories that are showcased on your GitHub Sponsors profile.
         <ul> 
          <li>To the right of "Featured work", click <strong>Edit</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/featured-work-edit-button.png" alt="Edit button for featured work"></span></li> 
          <li>Select up to 6 repositories, then click <strong>Save</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/featured-work-select.png" alt="Checkboxes to select repositories"></span></li> 
         </ul></li> 
        <li>Optionally, select <strong>Opt-in to potentially being featured on github.com/sponsors</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/opt-in-to-being-featured.png" alt="Checkbox to opt-in to being featured"></span></li> 
        <li>Click <strong>Update profile</strong>.</li> 
       </ol> 
       <h3 id="creating-sponsorship-tiers"><a href="#creating-sponsorship-tiers">Creating sponsorship tiers</a></h3> 
       <p>You can create up to 10 one-time sponsorship tiers and 10 monthly tiers for sponsors to choose from. Each tier has its own one-time or monthly payment amount in US dollars, and you can choose to enable tiers for custom amounts. In addition, you can choose to enable tiers for custom amounts (monthly and one-time).</p> 
       <p>You can customize the rewards for each tier. For example, rewards for a tier could include:</p> 
       <ul> 
        <li>Early access to new versions </li> 
        <li>Logo or name in README</li> 
        <li>Access to a private repository</li> 
        <li>Weekly newsletter updates</li> 
        <li>Other rewards your sponsors would enjoy ? </li> 
       </ul> 
       <p>Once you have published a tier, you cannot edit the price of that tier. Instead, you must retire the tier and create a new tier. Existing sponsors will remain on the retired tier until they change their sponsorship tier, cancel their sponsorship, or their one-time sponsorship period expires.</p> 
       <p>The maximum price is US$12,000 per month.</p> 
       <ol> 
        <li>In the upper-right corner of any page, click your profile photo, then click <strong>Your sponsors</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/access-github-sponsors-dashboard.png" alt="GitHub Sponsors button"></span></li> 
        <li>If a list of your sponsored and eligible accounts is shown, to the right of the account you want to manage, click <strong>Dashboard</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/dev-sponsors-dashboard-button.png" alt="Developer sponsors dashboard button"></span></li> 
        <li>On the left sidebar, click <strong>Sponsor tiers</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/sponsor-tiers-tab.png" alt="Sponsor tiers tab"></span></li> 
        <li>To create a monthly tier, click <strong>Add a monthly tier</strong> at the right of the page. Alternatively, to create a tier for one-time payments, click <strong>One-time tiers</strong> and then click <strong>Add a one-time tier</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/add-a-tier-button.png" alt="Add a tier button"></span></li> 
        <li>Type a price per month and description for the tier. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/tier-price-description.png" alt="Tier price and description text fields"></span></li> 
        <li>Click <strong>Save draft</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/save-tier-draft.png" alt="Save draft button"></span></li> 
        <li>Proofread your tier, then click <strong>Publish <em>TYPE</em> tier</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/publish-tier-button.png" alt="Publish monthly tier button"></span></li> 
        <li>Repeat the steps above until you've published all the tiers you want to offer.</li> 
       </ol> 
       <h3 id="submitting-your-bank-information"><a href="#submitting-your-bank-information">Submitting your bank information</a></h3> 
       <p>As a sponsored organization, you must receive payouts to a dedicated bank account for your organization in a supported region. You can get a business bank account through services like <a href="https://opencollective.com/">Open Collective</a> and <a href="https://stripe.com/atlas">Stripe Atlas</a>. The region your organization legally operates in and the region of your bank account must match. The person setting up GitHub Sponsors for the organization must live in the same supported region, too. For a list of supported regions, see <a href="https://github.com/sponsors#countries">GitHub Sponsors</a>.</p> 
       <p>When applying for a Stripe Connect account, ensure all of your information is correct before you submit your application. It's difficult to update identity information, like your name and date of birth, after your application has been submitted.</p> 
       <ol> 
        <li>In the upper-right corner of any page, click your profile photo, then click <strong>Your sponsors</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/access-github-sponsors-dashboard.png" alt="GitHub Sponsors button"></span></li> 
        <li>If a list of your sponsored and eligible accounts is shown, to the right of the account you want to manage, click <strong>Dashboard</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/dev-sponsors-dashboard-button.png" alt="Developer sponsors dashboard button"></span></li> 
        <li>Click <strong>Stripe Connect account</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/create-stripe-connect-account.png" alt="Link to create Stripe Connect account"></span></li> 
        <li>Follow the prompts to create your account.</li> 
       </ol> 
       <p>For more information about setting up Stripe Connect using Open Collective, see <a href="https://docs.opencollective.com/help/collectives/github-sponsors">Setting up GitHub Sponsors</a> in the Open Collective Docs.</p> 
       <h3 id="submitting-your-tax-information"><a href="#submitting-your-tax-information">Submitting your tax information</a></h3> 
       <p>For more information about the tax form organizations need to complete, see <a href="https://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/iw8bene.pdf">Instructions for Form W-8BEN-E</a> on the United States Internal Revenue Service website.</p> 
       <ol> 
        <li>In the upper-right corner of any page, click your profile photo, then click <strong>Your sponsors</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/access-github-sponsors-dashboard.png" alt="GitHub Sponsors button"></span></li> 
        <li>If a list of your sponsored and eligible accounts is shown, to the right of the account you want to manage, click <strong>Dashboard</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/dev-sponsors-dashboard-button.png" alt="Developer sponsors dashboard button"></span></li> 
        <li>In the left sidebar, click <strong>Settings</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/settings-tab.png" alt="Settings tab"></span></li> 
        <li>Under "Country or region of residence", use the drop-down menu, and select your residential location. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/country-of-residence-drop-down.png" alt="Country or region of residence drop-down"></span></li> 
        <li>Click <strong>Save</strong>.</li> 
        <li>In the left sidebar, click <strong>Overview</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/overview-tab.png" alt="Overview tab"></span></li> 
        <li>Click <strong>tax form</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/tax-form-link.png" alt="Link to fill out a tax form"></span></li> 
        <li>Complete, sign, and submit the tax form.</li> 
       </ol> 
       <h3 id="enabling-two-factor-authentication-2fa-on-your-github-account"><a href="#enabling-two-factor-authentication-2fa-on-your-github-account">Enabling two-factor authentication (2FA) on your GitHub account</a></h3> 
       <p>Before your organization can become a sponsored organization, you must enable 2FA on your GitHub account. For more information, see "<a href="/en/articles/configuring-two-factor-authentication">Configuring two-factor authentication</a>."</p> 
       <h3 id="submitting-your-application-to-github-for-approval"><a href="#submitting-your-application-to-github-for-approval">Submitting your application to GitHub for approval</a></h3> 
       <ol> 
        <li>In the upper-right corner of any page, click your profile photo, then click <strong>Your sponsors</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/access-github-sponsors-dashboard.png" alt="GitHub Sponsors button"></span></li> 
        <li>If a list of your sponsored and eligible accounts is shown, to the right of the account you want to manage, click <strong>Dashboard</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/dev-sponsors-dashboard-button.png" alt="Developer sponsors dashboard button"></span></li> 
        <li>Click <strong>Request approval</strong>. <span class="procedural-image-wrapper"><img src="/assets/images/help/sponsors/request-approval-button.png" alt="Request approval button"></span></li> 
       </ol> 
       <p>It may take a few days for GitHub to review your application. After we approve your application, your profile will automatically be live on <a href="https://github.com/sponsors">GitHub Sponsors</a>.</p>
