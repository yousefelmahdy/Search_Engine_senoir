                <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Although Matomo Analytics is a web analytics software with a purpose to track user activity on your website, we take privacy very seriously.&nbsp;</span></p>
                <p>The following advanced privacy protections in Matomo provide you with more control:</p>
                <ul>
                 <li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Matomo has an opt-out mechanism which </span><a href="https://matomo.org/faq/general/faq_20000/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">lets users opt-out of web analytics tracking</span></a></li>
                 <li>You can configure for data retention of raw data and aggregated reports</li>
                 <li><span style="font-weight: 400;"><a href="https://matomo.org/docs/privacy-how-to/#step-1-automatically-anonymize-visitor-ips">Anonymise IP addresses</a>&nbsp;as well as implementing other <a href="https://matomo.org/blog/2020/02/what-is-data-anonymization-in-web-analytics/">data anonymisation techniques</a></span></li>
                 <li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Respect </span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><a href="https://matomo.org/docs/privacy-how-to/#step-4-respect-donottrack-preference">DoNotTrack setting</a></span></li>
                 <li>Configure Matomo to <a href="https://matomo.org/blog/2018/04/how-to-not-process-any-personal-data-with-matomo-and-what-it-means-for-you/">not process any personal data or PII (personally identifiable information)</a></li>
                 <li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Set shorter expiration dates for tracking cookies</span></li>
                 <li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Disable Visits Log and Visitor Profile</span></li>
                 <li>Users arent tracked across websites unless specifically enabled</li>
                 <li>Matomo can be used without needing consent (unlike with Google, which will always need user consent). This strengthens privacy as it means youre not following visitors (e.g. over multiple days)</li>
                 <li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Read about the </span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><a href="https://matomo.org/blog/2020/05/11-ways-piwik-analytics-helps-protect-visitors-privacy/">12 ways Matomo helps you protect your visitors privacy</a></span></li>
