To ensure you receive a genuine Apple battery during a battery replacement, we recommend visiting an <a href="https://locate.apple.com/bh/en/" class="analytics-exitlink">Apple Authorized Service Provider</a>. If you need a replacement adapter to charge your Apple device, we recommend getting an Apple power adapter.</p>
