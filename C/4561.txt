          </div> <p>This is the main <b>directory of <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject">WikiProjects</a></b> and related <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Administration" title="Wikipedia:Administration">administration pages</a> on English Wikipedia. Due to the high volume of WikiProjects (over 2,000), this directory has been split into several "<a href="/wiki/Category:WikiProject_Council_directory" title="Category:WikiProject Council directory">sub-directories</a>" based on the projects' scope. You can also use the <a href="/wiki/Special:Search" title="Special:Search">Special:Search</a> box below to locate WikiProjects and sub-pages. </p>
