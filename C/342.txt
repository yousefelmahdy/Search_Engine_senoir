      <p>This page provides an <b>index of <a href="/wiki/Help:Template" title="Help:Template">templates</a></b> used within Wikipedia, many of which convey <i>messages</i>; they are grouped into topic-specific headings. For information on what templates are, and how the template namespace is intended to be used, see <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Template_namespace" title="Wikipedia:Template namespace">WP:Template namespace</a>. For information on template use and development, see <a href="/wiki/Help:Template" title="Help:Template">Help:Template</a>. For the project team looking after templates on Wikipedia, see <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Templates" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Templates">WP:WikiProject Templates</a>. </p>
      <p>For navigation templates rather than notices, see <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Navigation_templates" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:Navigation templates">WP:Navigation templates</a>. For stub templates (used to flag articles as incomplete and needing significant expansion), see <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Stub_sorting/Stub_types" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Stub sorting/Stub types">WP:WikiProject Stub sorting/Stub types</a>. For some examples of message templates, see <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Template_index/Examples" title="Wikipedia:Template index/Examples">WP:Template index/Examples</a>. For examples of combined message boxes, see <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Template_index/Cleanup/Verifiability_and_sources#Combined_message_boxes" title="Wikipedia:Template index/Cleanup/Verifiability and sources">WP:Template index/Cleanup/Verifiability and sources § Combined message boxes</a>. </p>

      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Using_this_page">Using this page</span></h2> 
      <p>The <b>bold links</b> at the top of each cell lead to detailed pages which describe the templates in that section in detail and explain how and when to use them. </p>
      <p>If you cannot find the template you are looking for, please click on the section headers that seem most relevant, since there may be templates for a particular usage covered on the detailed pages, but <b>not</b> listed in the table below. </p>
      <p>If you still cannot find the template you need, you can request it on <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Requested_templates" title="Wikipedia:Requested templates">Wikipedia:Requested templates</a>. For help on creating templates, see <a href="/wiki/Help:Template" title="Help:Template">Help:Template</a> or contact a user who has identified themself as an <a href="/wiki/Category:User_template_coder" title="Category:User template coder">adept template coder</a>. Note that in order to maintain consistent design of templates, there have been significant discussions about a variety of templates at both <a href="/wiki/Template_talk:Ambox" title="Template talk:Ambox">Template talk:Ambox</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia_talk:Talk_page_templates" title="Wikipedia talk:Talk page templates">Wikipedia talk:Talk page templates</a>. </p>
      <p>A more comprehensive category system may also be viewed at <a href="/wiki/Category:Wikipedia_templates" title="Category:Wikipedia templates">Category:Wikipedia templates</a>. </p> 
      <h3><span id="Can_improvements_be_made_easily_to_a_page.3F"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Can_improvements_be_made_easily_to_a_page?">Can improvements be made easily to a page?</span></h3> 
      <p>Before placing cleanup templates on a page, it is worthwhile to cast a <a href="/wiki/Critical_thinking" title="Critical thinking">critical</a> eye over the page to determine whether or not you can make the improvements easily, thus eliminating the need for a tag. </p>

       <li><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Template_index/Main_page_and_alternatives" title="Wikipedia:Template index/Main page and alternatives">Wikipedia:Template index/Main page and alternatives</a>  templates used on the <a href="/wiki/Main_Page" title="Main Page">Main Page</a>.</li> 
       <li><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Hatnote" title="Wikipedia:Hatnote">Wikipedia:Hatnote</a> and <a href="/wiki/Template:Hatnote_templates" title="Template:Hatnote templates">Template:Hatnote templates</a>  documentation for <a href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/hatnote" class="extiw" title="wikt:hatnote">hatnotes</a>.</li> 
       <li><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Template_index/Links#External_links" title="Wikipedia:Template index/Links">Wikipedia:Template index/Links § External links</a> and <a href="/wiki/Category:External_link_templates" title="Category:External link templates">Category:External link templates</a>  templates for links to <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:External_links" title="Wikipedia:External links">external sites</a>.</li> 
       <li><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Navigation_template" title="Wikipedia:Navigation template">Wikipedia:Navigation template</a> and <a href="/wiki/Category:Navigational_boxes" title="Category:Navigational boxes">Category:Navigational boxes</a>  guidance essay and list.</li> 
       <li><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Inline_templates_linking_country_articles" title="Wikipedia:Inline templates linking country articles">Wikipedia:Inline templates linking country articles</a>  templates that render a flag icon and a wikilink to a country's article, using a list of <a href="/wiki/Country_code" title="Country code">country codes</a>.</li> 
       <li><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:List_of_infoboxes" title="Wikipedia:List of infoboxes">Wikipedia:List of infoboxes</a> and <a href="/wiki/Category:Infobox_templates" title="Category:Infobox templates">Category:Infobox templates</a>  lists of templates for <a href="/wiki/Infobox" title="Infobox">infoboxes</a>, commonly used in articles to present certain summary or overview information about the subject.         <ul>
         <li><a href="/wiki/Template:Taxobox/doc" title="Template:Taxobox/doc">Template:Taxobox/doc</a>  template usage instructions for <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Tree_of_Life#Taxoboxes" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Tree of Life">taxoboxes</a> used for biological classification. See also <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Tree_of_Life" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Tree of Life">Wikipedia:WikiProject Tree of Life</a> for project overview.</li>
        </ul></li> 
       <li><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Countering_systemic_bias#Related_cleanup_templates" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Countering systemic bias">Wikipedia:WikiProject Countering systemic bias § Related cleanup templates</a> and <a href="/wiki/Category:Internationalisation_maintenance_templates" title="Category:Internationalisation maintenance templates">Category:Internationalisation maintenance templates</a>  templates for noting a particular national or regional bias of an article and also for asking for a <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WORLDVIEW" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:WORLDVIEW">worldwide view</a>.</li> 
       <li><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Stub_sorting/Stub_types" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Stub sorting/Stub types">Wikipedia:WikiProject Stub sorting/Stub types</a>  templates that are applied to <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Stub" title="Wikipedia:Stub">stubs</a>, from a WikiProject to list stub messages/templates.</li>
