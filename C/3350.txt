           </div> The <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Spam_blacklist" title="Special:MyLanguage/Spam blacklist">associated page</a> is used by the <a href="https://www.mediawiki.org/wiki/" class="extiw" title="mw:">MediaWiki</a> <a href="https://www.mediawiki.org/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Extension:SpamBlacklist" class="extiw" title="mw:Special:MyLanguage/Extension:SpamBlacklist">Spam Blacklist extension</a>, and lists <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/regular_expressions" class="extiw" title="w:Special:MyLanguage/regular expressions">regular expressions</a> which cannot be used in URLs in any page in Wikimedia Foundation projects (as well as many external wikis). Any <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Meta:Administrators" title="Special:MyLanguage/Meta:Administrators">Meta administrator</a> can edit the spam blacklist; either manually or with <a href="/wiki/User:Erwin/SBHandler" title="User:Erwin/SBHandler">SBHandler</a>. For more information on what the spam blacklist is for, and the processes used here, please see <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Spam_blacklist/About" title="Special:MyLanguage/Spam blacklist/About">Spam blacklist/About</a>. <p><ins id="header"></ins><ins id="guidance"></ins> </p> 
           <dl>
            <dt>
             <a href="#Proposed_additions">Proposed additions</a>
            </dt> 
            <dd>
             Please provide evidence of spamming on several wikis. <span style="color:red;">Spam that only affects a single project should go to that project's local blacklist.</span> Exceptions include malicious domains and URL redirector/shortener services. Please follow <a class="external text" href="https://meta.wikimedia.org/w/index.php?title=Talk:Spam_blacklist&amp;diff=1240123&amp;oldid=1240112">this</a> format. Please check back after submitting your report, there could be questions regarding your request.
            </dd> 
            <dt>
             <a href="#Proposed_removals">Proposed removals</a>
            </dt> 
            <dd>
             <i>Please</i> check our <a href="/wiki/Spam_blacklist/Recurring_requests" class="mw-redirect" title="Spam blacklist/Recurring requests">list</a> of requests which repeatedly get declined. Typically, we do not remove domains from the spam blacklist in response to site-owners' requests. Instead, we de-blacklist sites when trusted, high-volume editors request the use of blacklisted links because of their value in support of our <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wikimedia_projects" title="Special:MyLanguage/Wikimedia projects">projects</a>. Please consider whether requesting whitelisting on a specific wiki for a specific use is more appropriate - that is <i>very</i> often the case.
            </dd>
           </dl> 
           <dl>
            <dt>
             Other discussion
            </dt> 
            <dd>
             <i><a href="#Troubleshooting_and_problems">Troubleshooting and problems</a></i> - If there is an error in the blacklist (i.e. a <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/regex" class="extiw" title="w:regex">regex</a> error) which is causing problems, please raise the issue here.
            </dd> 
            <dd>
             <i><a href="#Discussion">Discussion</a></i> - Meta-discussion concerning the operation of the blacklist and related pages, and communication among the spam blacklist team.
            </dd> 
            <dd>
             <i><bdi dir="ltr" id="wikimedia-external-links"><a href="irc://irc.libera.chat/wikimedia-external-links" class="extiw" title="irc:wikimedia-external-links"><bdi lang="zxx" dir="ltr" style="font-family: monospace,Courier; white-space: nowrap;">#wikimedia-external-links</bdi></a><sup lang="en" dir="auto" class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://web.libera.chat/?channel=#wikimedia-external-links"><span style="color: green; text-decoration: inherit; -moz-text-decoration-color: green; text-decoration-color: green;">connect</span></a></sup></bdi></i> - Real-time <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/IRC" title="Special:MyLanguage/IRC">IRC</a> chat for co-ordination of activities related to maintenance of the blacklist.
            </dd> 
            <dd>
             <i><b>Whitelists</b></i>
             <br>There is no global whitelist, so if you are seeking a whitelisting of a url at a wiki then please address such matters via use of the respective <code>Mediawiki talk:Spam-whitelist</code> page at that wiki, and you should consider the use of the template {{edit protected}} or its local equivalent to get attention to your edit.
            </dd>
           </dl> 
           <hr> <p><i>Please <b>sign your posts</b> with ~~~~ after your comment.</i> This leaves a signature and timestamp so conversations are easier to follow. </p>

      <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="COIBot"><a href="/wiki/User:COIBot/XWiki" title="User:COIBot/XWiki">COIBot</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Talk:Spam_blacklist&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: COIBot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3> 
      <p>The LinkWatchers report domains meeting the following criteria: </p> 
      <ul>
       <li>When a user mainly adds this link, and the link has not been used too much, and this user adds the link to more than 2 wikis</li> 
       <li>When a user mainly adds links on one server, and links on the server have not been used too much, and this user adds the links to more than 2 wikis</li> 
       <li>If ALL links are added by IPs, and the link is added to more than 1 wiki</li> 
       <li>If a small range of IPs have a preference for this link (but it may also have been added by other users), and the link is added to more than 1 wiki.</li>
      </ul>
