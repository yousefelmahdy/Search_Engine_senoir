      <div class="faq__heading h-c-page"> 
       <h1 class="heading-1__sizing">Frequently asked questions</h1> 
       <p>Here are the answers to some of the most frequently asked questions about our data centers.</p> 
      </div> 
     </div> 
    </section> 
    <div class="glue-zippy-facade h-c-page" ng-cloak data-glue-zippy-facade-set-aria-hidden="true"> 
     <ul class="h-c-grid"> 
      <li id="zippy-facade-1" class="glue-a-zippy--v-smooth-expand h-c-grid__col h-c-grid__col--12 h-c-grid__col-l--12 banner__description"> 
       <div class="faq-question"> 
        <h2 class="heading-2__sizing">What is a data center?</h2> 
        <svg aria-hidden="true" role="presentation" class="h-c-icon--18px h-c-expansion-panels__button-arrow"> <use xlink:href="#mi-arrow-item"></use> 
        </svg> 
       </div> <p>A data center is a facility with many computers that store and process large amounts of information. Our data centers keep all of Google’s products and services up and running around the clock and around the world. Whenever you access <a href="https://mail.google.com/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Gmail</a>, edit your <a href="https://drive.google.com/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">documents</a>, or search for information on <a href="https://www.google.com/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Google</a>, you're using one of our data centers and have the power of a supercomputer at your fingertips. <a href="https://sustainability.google/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Learn more about our approach to environmentally responsible data centers</a>.</p> </li> 
      <li id="zippy-facade-2" class="glue-a-zippy--v-smooth-expand h-c-grid__col h-c-grid__col--12 h-c-grid__col-l--12 banner__description"> 
       <div class="faq-question"> 
        <h2 class="heading-2__sizing">Can I visit a data center?</h2> 
        <svg aria-hidden="true" role="presentation" class="h-c-icon--18px h-c-expansion-panels__button-arrow"> <use xlink:href="#mi-arrow-item"></use> 
        </svg> 
       </div> <p>Because we take <a href="/about/datacenters/data-security/">security</a> very seriously at our data centers, we cannot offer public tours at this time. However, you can watch our <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zDAYZU4A3w0" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">360° tour of our data center at The Dalles, Oregon</a> and <a href="/about/datacenters/gallery/">browse our photo gallery</a>.</p> </li> 
      <li id="zippy-facade-3" class="glue-a-zippy--v-smooth-expand h-c-grid__col h-c-grid__col--12 h-c-grid__col-l--12 banner__description"> 
       <div class="faq-question"> 
        <h2 class="heading-2__sizing">What's it like to work at the data center?</h2> 
        <svg aria-hidden="true" role="presentation" class="h-c-icon--18px h-c-expansion-panels__button-arrow"> <use xlink:href="#mi-arrow-item"></use> 
        </svg> 
       </div> <p>Google data center staff ensure that the facility’s computers are running at their highest speed and efficiency 24 hours a day, seven days a week. Our jobs require a variety of skills and backgrounds — from entry-level technology assistants to experienced data center managers. <a href="/about/datacenters/life/">Meet the amazing people behind the technology</a> that lets us build for everyone, and <a href="https://careers.google.com/jobs/results/?company=Google&amp;jlo=en_US&amp;q=%22data%20center%2a%22" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">browse our career opportunities</a>.</p> </li> 
      <li id="zippy-facade-4" class="glue-a-zippy--v-smooth-expand h-c-grid__col h-c-grid__col--12 h-c-grid__col-l--12 banner__description"> 
       <div class="faq-question"> 
        <h2 class="heading-2__sizing">How does Google power its data centers?</h2> 
        <svg aria-hidden="true" role="presentation" class="h-c-icon--18px h-c-expansion-panels__button-arrow"> <use xlink:href="#mi-arrow-item"></use> 
        </svg> 
       </div> <p>In 2017, Google achieved a major milestone: purchasing 100% renewable energy to match our annual electricity consumption for global operations, including our data centers and offices. Our large-scale procurement of wind and solar power is a cornerstone of our sustainability efforts, and has made Google the <a href="https://www.blog.google/outreach-initiatives/environment/meeting-our-match-buying-100-percent-renewable-energy/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">world’s largest corporate buyer of renewable energy</a>. Learn more about how <a href="/about/datacenters/renewable/">we're sourcing clean energy for a better future</a>.</p> </li> 
      <li id="zippy-facade-5" class="glue-a-zippy--v-smooth-expand h-c-grid__col h-c-grid__col--12 h-c-grid__col-l--12 banner__description"> 
       <div class="faq-question"> 
        <h2 class="heading-2__sizing">Can I take photos of the data center?</h2> 
        <svg aria-hidden="true" role="presentation" class="h-c-icon--18px h-c-expansion-panels__button-arrow"> <use xlink:href="#mi-arrow-item"></use> 
        </svg> 
       </div> <p>We’ve done the work for you! <a href="/about/datacenters/gallery/">Take a look inside Google’s data centers</a> around the world and see the technology, people, and places that keep our products online.</p> </li> 
      <li id="zippy-facade-6" class="glue-a-zippy--v-smooth-expand h-c-grid__col h-c-grid__col--12 h-c-grid__col-l--12 banner__description"> 
       <div class="faq-question"> 
        <h2 class="heading-2__sizing">I’m a member of the media and am interested in data centers.</h2> 
        <svg aria-hidden="true" role="presentation" class="h-c-icon--18px h-c-expansion-panels__button-arrow"> <use xlink:href="#mi-arrow-item"></use> 
        </svg> 
       </div> <p>We thank you for your interest. Please visit our <a href="https://www.blog.google/press/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Press Corner</a> or contact our press team at <a href="mailto:press@google.com" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">press@google.com</a>.</p> </li> 
     </ul>
