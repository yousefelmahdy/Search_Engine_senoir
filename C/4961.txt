    <h2 id="who-we-are">Who We Are</h2> 
    <p>The Sales team is led by the <a href="/job-families/sales/chief-revenue-officer/">Chief Revenue Officer (CRO)</a>. Reporting into this individual are the:</p> 
    <ul> 
     <li><a href="/job-families/sales/vp-enterprise-sales/">VP, Enterprise Sales</a></li> 
     <li><a href="/job-families/sales/vp-of-commercial-sales/">VP, Commercial Sales</a> (Mid-Market and SMB)</li> 
     <li><a href="/sales/vp-of-field-operations/">VP, Field Operations</a></li> 
     <li><a href="/job-families/sales/vp-of-customer-success/">VP, Customer Success</a></li> 
     <li><a href="/job-families/sales/vp-of-global-channels/">VP, Global Channels</a></li> 
     <li><a href="/job-families/alliances/vp-of-alliances/">VP, Alliances</a></li> 
    </ul> 
    <p>The Sales team is organized into Large, Mid-Market, and SMB segments. (Descriptions of Sales segments can be found in the <a href="/handbook/sales/field-operations/gtm-resources/#segmentation">Field Operations Handbook - Segmentation section</a>.)</p> 
    <p>The people working with each segment and their quota are:</p> 
    <ul> 
     <li>Large: <a href="/job-families/sales/strategic-account-leader/">Strategic Account Leader</a> (SAL)</li> 
     <li>Mid-Market: <a href="/job-families/sales/account-executive/">Mid-Market Account Executive</a> (MM AE)</li> 
     <li>SMB: <a href="/job-families/sales/smb-customer-advocate/">SMB Account Executive</a> (SMB AE)</li> 
    </ul> 
    <p>See the <a href="/company/team/org-chart/">GitLab org chart</a> for more information.</p> 
    <h2 id="how-we-work">How We Work</h2> 
    <p>The GitLab Sales team embodies our CREDIT values in every aspect of our work. Specifically, we constantly work to overperform and achieve strong <a href="/handbook/values/#results">results</a>. We prioritize <a href="/handbook/values/#efficiency">efficiency</a> in our processes and operations. We succeed because of <a href="/handbook/values/#transparency">transparent</a>, cross-functional <a href="/handbook/values/#collaboration">collaboration</a>. We are committed to hiring for and celebrating <a href="/handbook/values/#diversity-inclusion">diversity</a>, and we strive to create an environment of <a href="/handbook/values/#diversity-inclusion">inclusiveness and belonging</a> for all. And, we embrace a spirit of <a href="/handbook/values/#iteration">iteration</a>, understanding that we can always improve.</p> 
    <h3 id="sales-tech-stack">Sales Tech Stack</h3> 
    <p>For information regarding the tech stack at GitLab, please visit the <a href="/handbook/business-ops/tech-stack-applications/#tech-stack-applications">Tech Stack Applications page</a> of the Business Operations handbook where we maintain a quick reference table of who should have access to various tools and the designated owner for each to contact with questions. To see a full list of the tools that make up our tech stack, visit the <a href="/handbook/business-ops/tech-stack/">Tech Stack Details page</a>.</p> 
    <h3 id="collaborating-with-the-team">Collaborating with the Team</h3> 
    <p>Please <a href="/handbook/handbook-usage/#why-handbook-first">use the Handbook</a> as much as possible to document important information. You should also use the Handbook as a starting point for any questions. <a href="https://docs.google.com/document/d/1l5mfHMck914I1aJMOCX4lU2J2sImrloUpSm3Nb5Pa9U/edit">Read here</a> for guidance on how we take notes on the GitLab Sales team.</p> 
    <p><a href="/handbook/values/#dogfooding">Dogfood</a> GitLab whenever you can – to collaborate on ideas, gather feedback, organize projects, and more.</p> 
    <p>When you have to use office applications, always use <a href="https://gsuite.google.com/together/">G Suite applications</a> instead of Microsoft Office; for example, Google Sheets instead of Excel, Google Docs instead of Word, and Google Slides instead of Keynote or Powerpoint. Give the entire company editing rights whenever possible so everyone can contribute.</p> 
    <h3 id="communicating-with-the-team">Communicating with the Team</h3> 
    <ul> 
     <li>The Sales team primarily communicates via Slack in our day-to-day work. Our main team channels are <strong>#sales</strong>, our general channel, and <strong>#field-fyi</strong>, our read-only channel for official field team announcements. 
      <ul> 
       <li>Email is used in some segments where customer privacy dictates additional security measures.</li>
